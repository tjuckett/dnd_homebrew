# Current State

## Latest Known Situation
- The players have uncovered evidence that the Astral Conclave moved beyond elemental harvesting into humanoid essence extraction.
- The Null Bloom Facility remains a central source of intelligence, danger, and moral horror.
- Alden Wren knows how to overload the modified containment device, but doing so likely kills him.
- Bloomwell's five-floor design, worked-stone shell, and internal teleport matrix make it harder to assault directly than a normal hidden lab.
- Surviving tabaxi captives may still be inside the facility, including fugitives in the ducts and at least one psi-resistant detainee.
- The Conclave has already shipped weaker humanoid essence hearts north for unknown integration into larger systems.
- Tension between practical Conclave operators and ideological zealots is increasing under the strain of unstable research.
- Kira remains one of the few known experts capable of explaining or countering Conclave breach technology if the players can secure her trust.
- The workshop boundary that helped keep elementals out of humanoid lands appears to depend on trapped elemental power and may be weakening as those reserves are drained.
- Northern Conclave infrastructure increasingly looks designed for active comet capture, not simple observation, suggesting the harvest phase may be closer than assumed.
- The players have reached [[Asmar]], where construct civilization is split between [[Calyxis Forge]] and [[Heliox Prime]] under a fragile cold truce.
- The [[Astral Conclave]] is poised to target Asmar's mountain crystal supply and any Sovereign-era infrastructure connected to the [[Elemental Crucible]].
- One construct player character may be the lost Sovereign of Accord, but their memory remains fractured.

## Immediate Threats
- Expansion of essence-harvesting operations
- Movement of experimental devices north
- Loss of remaining Bloomwell prisoners before they can be rescued or questioned
- Failure of the workshop boundary as its elemental prisoners are exhausted
- Swamp corruption encroaching on elemental territory
- Discovery of player interference by Conclave leadership
- Collapse of the Asmar truce into renewed civil war
- External theft or sabotage of Asmar's finite crystal reserves

## Immediate Opportunities
- Rescue or recruit key prisoners and defectors
- Turn Bloomwell's surviving captives into witnesses against the Conclave
- Find or preserve Kira's research before the Conclave does
- Trace shipments north
- Target active workshops
- Trigger internal Conclave fractures
- Use Asmar's crisis to learn how planetary ley flow and elemental severance are affecting the whole setting
- Build alliances with construct powers before the Conclave does
