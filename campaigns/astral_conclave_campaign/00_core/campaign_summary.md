# Campaign Summary

## Premise
The Astral Conclave is exploiting elemental and void-derived power sources to reshape control over planar boundaries. Their work has progressed from elemental harvesting into humanoid essence extraction, producing unstable synthetic hearts and devices that threaten both the balance of nature and the political order of the world.

## Themes
- Balance vs control
- Technology vs elemental magic
- Institutional ambition vs individual conscience
- The cost of survival and progress
- Corruption spreading through systems, not just places

## Major Facts
- The Conclave uses void comet energy to create or empower **Astral Hearts**.
- Older records describe some early constructs as "Harvesters," a name tied to the first phase of the Construct Uprising before later Asmari identities and states emerged.
- Kira designed prototype technology connected to planar breach mechanics, including a rift engine and elemental tuning rods.
- Kira later fled the Conclave after learning its leadership intended to use that research in service of elemental extermination.
- A workshop network of 8 sites exists; 4 are active and help trap, drain, or redirect elemental power.
- The Null Bloom Facility is a 5-floor underground site used for suppression, extraction, and humanoid essence experimentation.
- Several humanoid essence-based hearts have already been created and sent north.
- Stormhaven is controlled by the Shadow Fangs, who covertly support Conclave logistics in exchange for pre-war technology and continued access to a portal.
- Aetheris Island serves as a fortified Conclave stronghold with automaton-heavy defenses and an observatory.
- At least part of the Conclave's northbound infrastructure appears tied to comet-tracking and tractor-beam style capture systems rather than passive observation alone.
- Beyond [[Talgin]], the construct continent of [[Asmar]] is locked in a cold civil truce over how to survive a worsening energy crisis.
- Asmar's finite crystal economy is partially sustained by the hidden [[Elemental Crucible]], a Sovereign-era recharge network now under quiet strain.
- The Elemental War ended only after constructs and humanoids fought together to close catastrophic planar breaches that neither side could survive.

## Current Campaign Pressure
- The Conclave is escalating.
- The swamp corruption continues to spread.
- Players are positioned to expose, disrupt, or accidentally accelerate multiple power systems at once.
- Conflict on Asmar may reveal that the world's broader planar damage is worsening far beyond Talgin.
