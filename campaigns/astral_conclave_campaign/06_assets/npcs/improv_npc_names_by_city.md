## Fast Use Notes
- Calyxis and Heliox names work best for constructs, officials, archivists, tacticians, and engineers.
- Northern Base names skew humanoid Conclave military, technical staff, and support crew.
- Falldrift, Srosa, and Wintervale names are built for Talgin frontier civilians, guards, merchants, hunters, and priests.
- Stormhaven names fit smugglers, enforcers, innkeepers, dockhands, spies, and tired locals.
- Stonehearth names fit earth elementals, ritualists, crafters, and defenders.
