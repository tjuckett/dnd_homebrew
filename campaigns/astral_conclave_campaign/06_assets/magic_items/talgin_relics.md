# Talgin Relics and Devices

## Purpose
This file tracks reusable Talgin-side relics, field devices, and elemental-era tools pulled from older import notes and retained only where they fit current canon.

## Use Notes
- These entries are intentionally compact and may still need encounter-level tuning.
- Older owner names or provenance claims are preserved only when they do not conflict with current canon.
- Conclave devices are often better treated as objective props, security systems, or restricted loot than ordinary shop inventory.

## Relics

### Aetherbind Wrist Device
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: None
- Description: A bracer-sized magitech relay keyed to Conclave comms traffic and secure teleport-circle routing.
- Effects:
- Can send brief voice transmissions to linked receivers in the same region.
- Stores several authorized teleport-circle signatures, but activation usually requires security clearance or matching relay infrastructure.
- Particularly valuable as an intelligence item because wiped logs, emergency traffic, and destination data may expose Conclave movement patterns.

### Elemental Suppression Bracer
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Wondrous item, wrist device
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A pre-war bracer built to neutralize or hinder rogue elementals, with interface complexity beyond most surviving portal tools.
- Effects:
- Helps suppress or disrupt elemental manifestations, bindings, or surges in close operational range.
- Can interface with specialized sync hardware to coordinate with advanced transport or containment systems.
- Most surviving portal devices are too crude to link with it directly, making compatible sync gear strategically valuable.
- Especially suited to Conclave operatives or researchers working around elemental capture infrastructure.

### Arc Carbine
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Martial ranged weapon
- Attunement: None
- Description: A compact magitech firearm that discharges compressed lightning through a crystal barrel.
- Effects:
- Standard shots deal lightning damage rather than piercing.
- Holds a limited internal charge reservoir before needing a reload or recharge cycle.
- Some models support a risky overcharge burst for a stronger shot at the cost of stability.

### Everburning Candle
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: None
- Description: A small eternal flame associated with Kira's workshops and old elemental research caches.
- Effects:
- Sheds steady light that cannot be extinguished by water, wind, or ordinary magical darkness.
- Reveals hidden markings, maps, or encoded notes left in certain Kira-linked facilities.
- Can interfere with the bond between a corrupted elemental and an attached heart or containment source, making it especially useful in research or rescue scenes.

### Eye of the Veil
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A platinum monocle with a faint violet shimmer, associated in older notes with Lord Vancair Duskwright.
- Effects:
- Grants darkvision out to `60 feet`, or extends existing darkvision by `30 feet`.
- Grants advantage on sight-based Perception and Insight checks.
- Cast `see invisibility` once per short rest.
- Cast `detect thoughts` once per long rest with `DC 15`.

### Bracers of the Final Ascent
- Rarity: Legendary
- Type: Wondrous item, bracers
- Attunement: Requires attunement by a creature of 10th level or higher
- Description: Ornate bracers etched with constellations and threaded with raw void crystal, forged as a last-days war relic.
- Effects:
- Once per day, activate an Ascendant Surge for `5 rounds`.
- During the surge, all six ability scores become `30`, the wearer is treated as level `20`, increases in size by one category, and radiates visible starlight.
- When the surge ends, the wearer falls unconscious and cannot be awakened for `24 hours` except by `wish`.
- Each use carries a cumulative death chance: `25%` on first use, `50%` on second, `75%` on third.
- Using the bracers before 10th level would further increase that death chance, but such a creature cannot normally attune to them.
- On death from the bracers, a `DC 20 Constitution` save determines whether the body remains or is reduced to cosmic dust.
- After three uses, the bracers become inert for that wearer.
- GM note: best treated as a late-campaign emergency relic or catastrophic temptation rather than standard player loot.

### Heartroot Seed
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement by a druid
- Description: A heavy living seed wrapped in moss and druidic vitality.
- Effects:
- Cast `entangle` at will without spending a spell slot.
- Once per long rest, plant it to create a temporary healing totem in a `10-foot radius`.
- The totem can provide cover and absorb a limited amount of damage meant for nearby allies.
- Desecrated or corrupted ground can temporarily exhaust the seed until it is restored through druidic ritual.

## Elemental-Era Devices

### Elemental Detection Ward
- Type: Conclave security device
- Description: A rune-inscribed perimeter machine that detects elementals and triggers a preloaded binding or control response.
- Use:
- Best treated as a location defense rather than portable treasure.
- Activates in response windows, creating predictable but short gaps that clever infiltrators can exploit.
- Common at Conclave sites guarding portals, labs, or high-value containment infrastructure.

### Elemental Orbs
- Type: Condensed power cores
- Description: Dense elemental-energy spheres derived from captured or compressed elemental essence.
- Use:
- Can empower spellcasters, weapons, armor, constructs, or workshop-scale machinery.
- Closely tied to Kira's early war-era research and later prototype disasters.
- Existing surviving orbs should be treated as rare, politically dangerous, and morally compromising artifacts rather than routine fuel cells.
