# Asmar Construct Relics

## Purpose
This file tracks reusable construct-themed magic items, museum-grade relics, and rune upgrades tied to [[Asmar]], the [[Elemental Crucible]], and the Sovereign-era past.

## Use Notes
- These entries are adapted from the design notes in `chatgpt_conversations/asmar.txt`.
- Most items assume a construct-friendly magitech aesthetic and may need tuning for final table balance.
- "Construct only" can mean a construct creature, a construct player lineage, or a similarly appropriate synthetic character at GM discretion.

## Very Rare and Legendary Relics

### Nexus Core Prism
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement by a construct
- Description: A floating polyhedral crystal that hums with shifting elemental color.
- Effects:
- Grants `+2 AC` through a force barrier.
- Once per long rest, release a Nexus Pulse in a `30-foot radius`. Creatures in the area must make a `DC 18 Constitution` saving throw or take `6d10 force damage`, or half as much on a success.
- While attuned by a construct, the bearer is immune to the `charmed` and `frightened` conditions.

### Sovereign's Stabilizing Crown
- Rarity: Legendary
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: The prototype crown used by early awakened constructs to reinforce cognition and identity.
- Effects:
- Grants advantage on Intelligence, Wisdom, and Charisma saving throws.
- Once per day, cast `time stop` with its normal variable duration.
- If worn by a construct, the wearer can restore half their hit points as an action once per day.
- If worn by a construct, the wearer is immune to psychic damage.

### Arc-Spiral Great Hammer
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Weapon
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A heavy hammer bristling with elemental coils and old war-era capacitors.
- Effects:
- On a hit, it deals an extra `2d10 thunder` and `2d10 lightning` damage.
- Once per short rest, the wielder can slam the hammer into the ground to cast `chain lightning` with `DC 18`.
- If attuned by a construct, its critical hit range expands to `19-20`.

### Nullflare Repeater
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Ranged weapon
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A pre-war arcane firearm designed to disrupt elementals and planar traversal.
- Effects:
- Range `120/480`.
- Holds `6 charges` and regains `1d4 + 2` charges daily.
- A standard attack deals `3d10 radiant` plus `3d10 force` damage.
- By spending `2 charges`, the wielder fires a Nullflare Bolt that suppresses teleportation and planar travel in a `20-foot radius` for `1 minute`.

### Crucible Heart Engine
- Rarity: Legendary
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement by a construct
- Description: A beating geometric core infused with ley-line resonance and old Crucible harmonics.
- Effects:
- Grants resistance to all damage.
- Grants `+3` to all saving throws.
- Once per long rest, unleash a Crucible Burst in a `60-foot radius`. Creatures must make a `DC 20 Constitution` saving throw or take `8d12 radiant damage` and be stunned for `1 round`.
- Drawback: activating its deeper functions makes the bearer detectable by all nearby [[Elemental Crucible]] nodes within `1 mile`.

### Elemental Polyframe Chassis
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Armor
- Attunement: Requires attunement by a construct
- Description: A modular armor matrix that shifts elemental channels across its frame.
- Effects:
- Functions as `+2 half plate`.
- Does not impose disadvantage on Stealth checks.
- As a bonus action, choose resistance to `fire`, `cold`, `lightning`, or `acid` for `1 hour`.
- When the bearer takes damage of the chosen type, they can retaliate for `2d10` damage of that same type once per turn.

### Ley-Synchronous War Pike
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Weapon
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A spear tuned to planar frequency rather than simple metallurgy.
- Effects:
- Deals an extra `2d8 force` damage on hit.
- On hit, the target must make a `DC 17 Wisdom` saving throw or become partially phased for `1 round`.
- A phased target cannot make reactions and has its speed halved.
- Once per day, the wielder can cast `steel wind strike`.

### Aurora Shard Mantle
- Rarity: Legendary
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A mantle of refracted prismatic light held together by shard lattices rather than cloth.
- Effects:
- Grants `+3 AC`.
- Once per day, activate a Prismatic Barrier for `1 minute`. During that time, creatures that hit the wearer in melee take `5d10` damage of a random prismatic type.
- The wearer can also cast `prismatic spray` once per day.

### Sovereign's Echo Core
- Rarity: Very Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement by a construct
- Description: A humming sphere containing residual command authority from before the partition of Asmar.
- Effects:
- Grants `+2` to attack rolls and spell attack rolls.
- Once per long rest, cast either `mass suggestion` or `heal` as though `heal` were cast at 8th level.
- Unique feature: once ever, the attuned construct may issue a Sovereign Override to a construct creature. The target automatically fails the relevant save and is charmed for `1 minute`, ignoring normal charm resistance.
- Narrative note: Heliox would treat use of this power as proof that old authority can still become coercive.

### Solaris-Lattice Glaive
- Rarity: Legendary
- Type: Weapon
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A polearm forged during the Elemental War using solar-planar lattice work.
- Effects:
- On a hit, it deals an extra `2d10 radiant` and `2d10 fire` damage.
- While attuned, the wielder emits bright light for `40 feet` and dim light for another `40 feet`.
- Once per day, cast `sunburst` with `DC 20`.
- Once per day, when reduced to `0 hit points`, the wielder erupts in solar energy dealing `10d10 radiant damage` around them, then stabilizes at `1 hit point`.

## Rare and Uncommon Items

### Pulse-Thread Repair Spool
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Consumable
- Restriction: Construct only
- Description: A metallic spool of self-weaving repair threads.
- Effects:
- As an action, restore `3d6 + 3` hit points to a construct.
- If used during a long rest, the target's hit point maximum increases by `5` until the next rest.

### Echo-Lens Visual Array
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Wondrous item
- Description: A detachable optical module that enhances vision.
- Effects:
- Grants advantage on Perception checks relying on sight.
- Grants darkvision out to `120 feet`.

### Cold-Sink Bracer
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Wondrous item
- Description: A bracer that shunts thermal spikes into a self-cooling matrix.
- Effects:
- Reduces incoming fire damage by `5`.
- After reducing fire damage this way, the next melee hit the wearer makes deals an extra `1d6 cold` damage.

### Kinetic Recursion Loop
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Wondrous item
- Description: A wrist-mounted energy feedback loop.
- Effects:
- Once per turn, after the wearer takes melee damage, the attacker takes `1d4 lightning` damage.

### Resonant Communication Node
- Rarity: Uncommon
- Type: Wondrous item
- Description: A short-range pre-war transmission device.
- Effects:
- Allows telepathic communication with willing creatures within `300 feet`.
- No shared language is required.

### Crystal-Edged Repeater Blade
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Weapon, any sword
- Description: A blade lined with resonance crystal strands.
- Effects:
- Becomes a `+1` magical weapon.
- Deals an extra `1d6 radiant` damage on hit.
- Once per long rest, one hit can instead deal an extra `3d6 radiant` damage.

### Arc-Pulse Stabilizer
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Restriction: Construct only
- Description: A power distributor mounted near the heart-core.
- Effects:
- Grants resistance to lightning damage.
- Once per day, when the wearer fails a Dexterity saving throw, they may choose to succeed instead.

### Temporal Drift Capacitor
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Description: An unstable device storing micro-temporal charge.
- Effects:
- Once per day, grants an extra bonus action on the wearer's turn.
- After use, roll a `d20`.
- On `1-2`, the wearer is stunned for `1 round`.
- On `3-20`, no drawback occurs.

### Modular Shield Armulator
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Wondrous item
- Attunement: Requires attunement
- Description: A collapsible shield frame integrated into an arm assembly.
- Effects:
- Grants `+2 AC` while deployed.
- As a reaction `2/day`, reduce ranged or spell damage by `2d10`.
- Does not occupy the hand slot for wielding weapons.

### Flame-Cycle Hammer Module
- Rarity: Rare
- Type: Weapon upgrade
- Description: A crystalline socket meant for a hammer, maul, or mace.
- Effects:
- Makes the host weapon magical.
- Once per turn on hit, choose to deal an extra `1d8 fire` or `1d8 thunder` damage.
- Overcharge `1/day`: create a `10-foot` shockwave dealing `3d8 fire` or `3d8 thunder` damage, `Strength save DC 15` for mitigation or avoidance at GM discretion.

## Calyxis Market and Restricted Gear

### Standard Market Gear
- Flux-Thread Reinforcement Plates: `250 gp`. As a bonus action, grant `+1 AC` for `1 minute`, `2/day`.
- Resonant Actuator Core: `400 gp`. Gain advantage on one Strength or Dexterity check, `3/day`.
- Continuance Limb Modulators: `600 gp`. For `24 hours`, gain either `+5 feet movement` or `+1` to attack rolls with unarmed or melee weapons.
- Sensory Bloom Array: `350 gp`. Grants `blindsight 10 feet` and advantage on Perception checks against invisible creatures for `8 hours`.
- Minor Crystal Charge Cell: `75 gp`. Restores `2d6` hit points to a construct.
- Major Crystal Charge Cell: `200 gp`. Restores `5d6` hit points to a construct.
- Stabilized Ley Packet: `150 gp`. Ends `stunned`, `paralyzed`, or `restrained` on a construct.
- Thermal Flux Capsule: `60 gp`. The next weapon hit deals an extra `1d6 fire` or `1d6 cold`.
- Crystalline Harmonic Tuner: `180 gp`. Grants advantage on checks to stabilize or identify damaged crystals or mining equipment.
- Arc-Thread Repair Kit: `50 gp`. Supports a `DC 15` repair action that restores `1d8` hit points once per short rest.
- Ley-Flux Scanner: `220 gp`. Functions like `detect magic` at will, but only for ley-line-related phenomena.
- Kinetic Dampening Harness: `300 gp`. Grants resistance to falling damage and forced movement effects for `24 hours`.
- Energized Maul Capacitor: `250 gp`. Attached to a hammer or maul, it adds `2d8 lightning` damage to one hit `1/day`.
- Crystal-Edge Blade Module: `200 gp`. Makes a slashing weapon magical and grants `+1` slashing damage for `24 hours`.
- Shock-Burst Grenade, Calyxis Model C: `125 gp`. Deals `3d6 lightning` damage in a burst, `Dexterity save DC 14` for half.

### Service Offerings
- Memory Stream Defragmentation: `150 gp`. Functions like `lesser restoration` and grants advantage on the next Intelligence check.
- Chassis Stress Realignment: `200 gp`. Removes one level of exhaustion.
- Continuance Upgrade Consultation: `50 gp`. Grants a temporary `+1` bonus to the next attack roll or skill check.
- Sovereign-Era Interface Testing: `300 gp`. Offered only with trust and likely only to the construct PC; may trigger a memory echo.

### Restricted Goods
- Proto-Crystal Core Shard: `900 gp`. Causes armor or a weapon to become `+1` for `1 hour`, but carries a `5%` chance of an unstable surge that deals `2d6 force` damage to the user.
- Ley Infusion Rod: `1,200 gp`. Once per long rest, casts `haste` on a construct.

## Museum Artifacts

### The First Resonance Crown
- Description: A delicate circlet of crystalline filaments designed to amplify early construct cognition.
- Historical Importance: Worn by the first generation of awakened constructs before the Sovereign unified them.
- Secret: It faintly vibrates when near the construct PC.

### The Sovereign's Unfinished Mandate Tablet
- Description: A stone slab carrying partial directives meant for a council meeting that never occurred.
- Historical Importance: The last known public writing of the Sovereign before departure.
- Surviving fragment: "Stability is not survival. Survival is not purpose."

### Elemental War Armature, Mark VI
- Description: A massive gauntlet scarred by fire and frost from battles against elemental titans.
- Historical Importance: Proof that constructs once fought alongside humanity during the Elemental War.

### The Accord Beacon
- Description: A small crystalline obelisk that once pulsed when peace was formally enacted between [[Asmar]] and [[Talgin]].
- Historical Importance: Marks the moment the Construct Accord became real.
- Current note: Its light has dimmed over the last few decades.

### Pre-Partition Seal Key
- Description: One of the original security tokens used to access unified construct command networks.
- Historical Importance: Once limited to the Sovereign and early high command.
- Secret: Dormant glyphs glow in the construct PC's proximity.

### The Ley-Flux Compass
- Description: A pre-war navigational instrument built to map ley lines during the Elemental War.
- Historical Importance: Recorded several conduits now believed lost.
- Current behavior: Spins erratically in a way that may reflect waning ley flow.

### The Heartfire Conduit Core
- Description: A chunk of an elemental reactor once used to purify corrupted terrain.
- Historical Importance: Credited with stabilizing dangerous postwar regions.
- Current behavior: Still warm to the touch despite the passing ages.

### Memory Archive Theta-Prime
- Description: A self-contained crystalline library holding recorded memories of early awakened constructs.
- Historical Importance: Preserves the first debates over autonomy, survival, and identity.
- Function: Can project historical scenes modern systems cannot fully interpret.

### Null-Break Bowstring
- Description: A filament from an early anti-elemental weapon tuned to planar-disrupting frequencies.
- Historical Importance: Used during the final confrontations of the Elemental War.
- Rumor: Some accounts claim the Sovereign carried one during the Uprising.

### The Original Continuance Prototype
- Description: A small metallic cube from the first experiment in adaptive self-modification.
- Historical Importance: Foreshadows what became the Calyxis doctrine.
- Current behavior: It mirrors the structure of whoever touches it, except the construct PC, for whom it forms an unknown geometric pattern.

## Rune Upgrades

### Standard Magical Runes
- Rune of Overload Pulse: Weapon or chassis rune. Once per turn on a melee hit, deal an extra `1d8 lightning` damage. Once per long rest, emit a pulse wave in `10 feet`; creatures make a Constitution save or take `3d8 lightning`.
- Rune of Reinforced Weave: Armor or chassis rune. Grants `+1 AC`, and `1/day` grants resistance to force damage for `1 minute`.
- Rune of Thermal Shift: Weapon rune. Each attack can deal an extra `1d6 fire` or `1d6 cold`. If the user alternates types on consecutive turns, they gain advantage on the next attack.
- Rune of Echo-Step: Mobility rune. Cast `misty step` `1/day` without components, and after teleporting gain `+10 feet movement` until end of turn.
- Rune of Harmonic Guard: Defensive rune. As a reaction when hit, impose disadvantage on the triggering attack `2/day`. If the attack still hits, gain `5 temporary hit points`.
- Rune of Ley Resonance: Weapon or implement rune. On a hit with a weapon or spell attack, a construct bearer regains `1d4` hit points. Once per long rest, cast `shield` as a reaction.
- Rune of Gravity Fold: Weapon or utility rune. Melee attacks pull a Large or smaller target `5 feet` closer on hit. `1/day`, cast `earthbind` without verbal components.
- Rune of Amplified Stride: Mobility rune. Grants `+10 feet` walking speed and doubled jump distance. As a bonus action `1/day`, Dash without provoking opportunity attacks.
- Rune of Echo Memory: Utility or chassis rune. Grants advantage on Intelligence checks and saves against illusions. `1/day`, triggers a GM-defined flash of clarity or recovered detail.
- Rune of Cyclonic Impact: Weapon rune. On a critical hit, all creatures within `10 feet` make a Dexterity save or take `2d6 thunder` damage and be pushed `10 feet`. Once per long rest, force a creature hit by the user to fall prone.

### Health-Powered Runes
- Bloodspark Rune: Costs `5 HP`. The next weapon hit deals an extra `1d8 lightning`.
- Ironlash Rune: Costs `5 HP`. Gain `+2` to attack rolls until the end of the turn.
- Forceguard Rune: Costs `8 HP`. Gain `+2 AC` until the start of the next turn.
- Momentum Rune: Costs `5 HP`. Immediately move `10 feet` without provoking opportunity attacks.
- Echo-Shield Rune: Costs `6 HP`. Reduce the next damage taken by `1d10`.
- Surgefire Rune: Costs `7 HP`. The next weapon or spell damage roll deals an extra `2d6 fire` or `2d6 cold`.
- Sync-Pulse Rune: Costs `5 HP`. Gain advantage on the next saving throw.
- Warpstep Rune: Costs `7 HP`. Teleport up to `10 feet` to an unoccupied space you can see.
- Spikeburst Rune: Costs `10 HP`. Creatures within `5 feet` take `1d6 force` damage.
- Recall Rune: Costs `3 HP`. Re-roll a failed ability check, attack roll, or saving throw, taking the new result.
