# Astral Conclave

## Goal
Centralize control over unstable forces by mastering planar technology, essence conversion, and ancient or pre-war systems.

## Methods
- Elemental harvesting
- Humanoid essence extraction
- Artifact recovery
- Fortress-scale magitech deployment
- Proxy logistics through criminal intermediaries

## Internal Friction
- Pragmatists want controlled progress and secrecy
- Zealots see moral compromise as weakness
- Technologists underestimate spiritual and ecological backlash
