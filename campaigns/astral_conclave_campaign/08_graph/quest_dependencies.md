# Quest Dependencies

## Null Bloom Infiltration
- Depends on knowledge of [[Astral Conclave]]
- Strongly connected to [[Null Bloom Facility]]
- May unlock clues about [[Northern Base]]
- Can affect [[Alden Wren]], [[Director Solenne Varn]], [[Inquisitor Taelix Dren]], [[Kasha "Whisperclaw"]], [[Mira "Silent Step"]], and [[Jinx "Nightwhisker"]]

## Comet-Harvest Arc
- Depends on understanding [[Astral Conclave]] comet operations on [[Aetheris Island]]
- May involve [[Kira]], [[Orion]], and [[Vesper "Pinkshade" Arlin]]
- Strongly connected to [[Astral Heart System]] and wider [[Planar Mechanics]]

## Swamp Corruption
- Depends on the corruption-removal precedent with earth elementals
- Begins through [[Eldara Stonewise]] of [[Stonehearth]]
- Threatens the safety of [[Stonehearth]]
- Connected to [[Elemental Corruption]]
- Centered on [[The Technomire]]
- May uncover the buried ruins of [[Valtoria]]
- May reveal deeper ties to [[Astral Heart System]], [[Kira]], or past Conclave activity

## Shattered Gears of Asmar
- Begins when the party reaches [[Asmar]] and warns [[Calyxis Forge]] about the [[Astral Conclave]]
- Depends on first contact with [[Magistra Pyra-IX]] and the city's need for neutral defenders
- Immediately pressures the fragile truce between [[Calyxis Forge]] and [[Heliox Prime]]
- Can reveal the function and limits of the [[Elemental Crucible]]
- May expose ties between Asmar's failing ley recharge and wider [[Planar Mechanics]]
- Can reshape construct politics if the Sovereign of Accord's identity becomes public
- May include a pursuit-and-boarding encounter against [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]] if Heliox escalates to overt mobile crystal seizure
