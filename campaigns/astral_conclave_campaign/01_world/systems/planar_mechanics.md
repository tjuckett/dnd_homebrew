# Planar Mechanics

## Current World Assumption
Planar boundaries are not naturally static; they can be stressed, sealed, pierced, or tuned.

## Kira's Work
Kira's rift engine prototype was designed to break planar seals at specific portal sites. It requires elemental tuning rods calibrated to the relevant planar signatures.
Kira later broke with the [[Astral Conclave]] after concluding its leadership intended to scale this work into planetary-level anti-elemental violence rather than limited containment or controlled transit.

## Tuning Rods
- One rod per relevant element or planar resonance
- Improper tuning causes instability, misalignment, or catastrophic bleed-through
- Calibration is part science, part magical resonance practice
- Surviving calibration notes may still exist in [[Kira's Hidden Lab]] or other abandoned research stores tied to her work

## Consequences of Failure
- Local corruption
- Energy surges
- Elemental madness
- Portal collapse or misfire
