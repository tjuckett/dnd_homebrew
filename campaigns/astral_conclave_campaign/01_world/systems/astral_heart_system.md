# Astral Heart System

## Definition
Astral Hearts are artificial power cores tied to void comet energy and essence extraction. They are used to fuel large-scale magitech systems including planar manipulation, suppression arrays, and fortress-scale devices.

## Sources
- Elemental essence
- Humanoid essence (weaker, more volatile, morally catastrophic)
- Void comet energy used as amplifier or catalytic stabilizer

## Behaviors
- Powerful but unstable when poorly tuned
- Can be specialized for containment, breach, propulsion, or suppression
- Improper balancing risks cascade failure, corruption spread, or mass life-drain

## Narrative Consequences
- The more the Conclave industrializes essence conversion, the more the process shifts from rare artifact creation into a scalable atrocity.
- Humanoid essence hearts are weaker than elemental hearts, but easier to mass-produce under cruel enough conditions.
