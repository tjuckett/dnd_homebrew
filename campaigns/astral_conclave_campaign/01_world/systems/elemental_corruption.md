# Elemental Corruption

## Nature
Corruption behaves like a spreading contamination of essence, resonance, and behavior. It distorts living elementals, nearby land, and magical systems.

## Symptoms
- Madness or compulsive aggression in elementals
- Terrain warping
- Magical instability
- Shared energy signatures with Conclave-linked devices

## Known Case
[[The Technomire]] has the same energy signature as the corrupted heart, suggesting a linked source or derived process. Its corruption appears tied both to the fallen systems of [[Valtoria]] and to an older elemental-harnessing prototype left to decay at the swamp's core.
