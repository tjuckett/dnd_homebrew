# Workshop Network

## Overview
A distributed network of 8 workshops, with 4 currently active, tied to elemental trapping, defense, energy routing, and the maintenance of the wider boundary that keeps most elementals from crossing freely into humanoid lands.

## Functions
- Attract or contain elementals
- Drain and store elemental power using elemental orbs
- Support local defenses while secretly feeding Conclave research
- Reinforce a regional anti-elemental boundary originally associated with Kira's hidden workshop system

## Strategic Truth
Removing or disabling the wrong node can save trapped elementals while also collapsing protective systems around innocent communities.

## Hidden Cost
- The workshop orbs are powered by captured elementals rather than clean ambient energy
- As those prisoners are slowly drained, the broader boundary network weakens over time
- The same system that has kept many settlements alive is also one of the clearest long-term atrocities built into Conclave-adjacent infrastructure

## Narrative Pressure
- Players may discover that preserving safety in the short term can require sustaining a cruel system they would rather destroy
- If the network begins to fail faster than expected, elemental incursions may return to regions that have mistaken enforced absence for peace
- Kira's legacy is morally compromised here: the workshops helped contain a larger disaster, but they did so by normalizing capture, extraction, and technological domination of elemental life

## Discovery Hooks
- Eternal candles reveal workshop locations when properly illuminated
- Robotic guardians contain drainable fluid that can power the reveal process
