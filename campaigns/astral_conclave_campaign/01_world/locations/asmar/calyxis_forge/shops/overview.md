# Calyxis Forge Services

## Summary
Calyxis Forge's markets are not built around comfort goods. They revolve around repair, optimization, energy rationing, and the ugly fact that scarcity has become part of everyday civic life.

## Notable Shops and Services
- The Lattice Exchange: Energy-allocation market where constructs barter for processing priority, recharge access, and municipal efficiency credits
- Forge of Continuance: Adaptive fabrication complex selling modular limbs, crystal-compatible chassis parts, and battlefield-grade retrofit packages
- The Rebinding Crucible: Controlled repair hall offering structural reinforcement, memory defragmentation, and post-trauma recalibration
- The Quiet Coil: Recovery chambers for damaged constructs that suppress harmful thought loops and overclocking spirals
- Cognition Vault Annex: Restricted museum wing where approved buyers or researchers can study pre-war artifacts and replica tools

## Sample Goods
- Minor Crystal Charge Cell: Construct-only repair consumable, `75 gp`
- Major Crystal Charge Cell: Stronger repair consumable, `200 gp`
- Stabilized Ley Packet: Ends `stunned`, `paralyzed`, or `restrained` on a construct, `150 gp`
- Crystalline Harmonic Tuner: Grants advantage on checks involving damaged crystals or mining equipment, `180 gp`
- Ley-Flux Scanner: Detects ley-line-related magic, `220 gp`
- Shock-Burst Grenade (Calyxis Model C): `3d6` lightning burst, `125 gp`
- Proto-Crystal Core Shard: Restricted temporary enhancement item with instability risk, `900 gp`
