# Calyxis Forge Hooks

## Summary
Calyxis Forge's hooks center on mine defense, controlled escalation, historical recovery, and the question of whether survival bought through adaptation is wisdom or desperation.

## Major Hooks
- Guard the High Spine Mine: [[Magistra Pyra-IX]] asks the party to defend Calyxis mining operations because sending more Calyxis troops would shatter the truce
- Stop the Reclaimer Heist: A Heliox mobile fortress steals mined crystals and attempts to outrun pursuit across the desert, forcing a boarding action under full movement
- Warning from Talgin: The party's report that the [[Astral Conclave]] intends to seize Asmar's energy supply forces Calyxis to treat outsiders as potential allies
- The Sovereign Returned: Ancient systems respond to the construct PC's resonance signature, but Calyxis refuses to decide whether that is salvation or merely a dangerous anomaly
- The Cognition Vaults: Museum artifacts and archive fragments may reveal who the Sovereign was before memory loss

## Escalations and Complications
- Heliox saboteurs strike the mine not to seize it, but to destroy its extraction stabilizers
- If the Reclaimer escapes with enough crystal mass, Heliox can claim both a strategic victory and fresh proof that Calyxis expansion invites stronger countermeasures
- The Crucible interface at the mine responds only to the construct PC, proving some of the oldest systems are not dead
- If the party visibly strengthens Calyxis, Heliox may interpret them as foreign mercenaries disguised as neutrals
- If the party does nothing, the Conclave may arrive to exploit both cities during the confusion

## Key Sites for Play
- The Ascendant Ring
- The Resonance Tier
- The Cognition Vaults
- The High Spine Mine
- The Deep Foundry
- Open desert pursuit lanes between the mines and Heliox-aligned withdrawal routes

## Outcome Pressure
- Saving the mine helps Calyxis in the short term but may deepen Heliox's fear that extraction is unsustainable
- Public revelation of the construct PC's identity could break the truce before the truth is understood
- Any sign that the Crucible can be altered may trigger a continent-wide scramble for control
