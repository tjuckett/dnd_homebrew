# Calyxis Forge NPCs

## Summary
Calyxis Forge's local cast is built around adaptive governance, strained infrastructure, and the practical choices required to keep a construct civilization alive under scarcity.

## Master NPC Files
- [[Magistra Pyra-IX]]: Leader of Calyxis Forge and chief advocate of the Continuance Doctrine

## Local Supporting Cast
- Warden-Guide Seris-9: Border patrol commander who first identifies the construct PC's pre-partition resonance signature
- Talan-Forge Khep-4: Senior mine engineer responsible for crystal yield analysis at the High Spine Mine
- Curator Ilyra-3: Keeper of the Cognition Vaults Museum and guardian of sensitive pre-war artifacts
- Rebinding Artisan Vel-Thread: Specialist in structural repair and memory defragmentation at the Rebinding Crucible
- Ledger-Mind Orax-11: Negotiator at the Lattice Exchange who tracks energy allotment conflicts and quiet hoarding

## Friction Points
- Seris-9 is loyal to Pyra-IX but distrusts any truth that arrives too late to be useful
- Khep-4 believes Heliox sabotage is real and imminent, but fears Calyxis is also underestimating long-term planetary risk
- Ilyra-3 suspects several museum pieces are keying off the construct PC, yet refuses to publicize it
