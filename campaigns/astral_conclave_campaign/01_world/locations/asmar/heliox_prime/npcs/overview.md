# Heliox Prime NPCs

## Summary
Heliox Prime's notable figures revolve around imposed stability, strict resource governance, and the belief that restraint is the only honest form of mercy left.

## Master NPC Files
- [[Arch-Calculant Veyrix]]: Leader of Heliox Prime and architect of the city's preservation-first doctrine

## Local Supporting Cast
- Severance Marshal Dhor-8: Covert field commander behind infrastructure-denial operations against Calyxis mining assets
- Archive Custodian Nemeris-4: Keeper of pre-partition executive records and one of the few constructs able to verify the Sovereign's old access class
- Quietus Prefect Sol-Vane: Civic official who oversees dormancy quotas and argues that decline can still be orderly
