# Stonehearth NPCs

## Summary
Stonehearth's leadership is concentrated in a small number of respected elemental figures who balance ritual life, defense, and communal endurance.

## Leadership
- [[Eldara Stonewise]]: High chieftain and oldest of the village leaders, marked by glowing runes that reflect her knowledge and authority
- [[Granitefist]]: Warrior chieftain whose strength and martial discipline anchor the village's defense
- [[Crystalmender]]: Healer and ritualist whose crystalline body and gemstone inlays reflect his role in restoration and spiritual care
