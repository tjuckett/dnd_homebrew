# Forbidden Lands

## Summary
The Forbidden Lands are a dangerous region of [[Talgin]] where elemental presence remains strong, travel is risky, and old wounds in the world have never fully healed. Settlements near this region treat it with caution, myth, and generational fear.

## Purpose
The Forbidden Lands serve as a regional home for Talgin's most dangerous elemental borderlands, corrupted landscapes, and hidden communities that endure beyond ordinary humanoid settlement patterns.

## Tone
Ancient, hostile, and half-understood. The region feels less conquered than survived around.
