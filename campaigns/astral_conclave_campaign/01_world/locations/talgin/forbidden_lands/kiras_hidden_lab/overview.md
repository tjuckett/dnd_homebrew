# Kira's Hidden Lab

## Summary
[[Kira's Hidden Lab]] is a decommissioned pre-war underwater research station hidden in the ocean west of the [[Forbidden Lands]]. Once built to study engineered sea life and hostile deep-water anomalies, it now serves as [[Kira]]'s isolated refuge, workshop, and archive.

## Purpose
The lab functions as a narrative refuge, safehouse, and knowledge site rather than a conventional dungeon. It gives the players access to one of the few people alive who understands the Conclave's breach technology from the inside and is actively trying to stop its worst endgame.

## Tone
Quiet, pressurized, and half-drowned. Most of the station feels abandoned to salt, darkness, and age, while the restored sections hum with improvised life support, salvage rigs, and Kira's relentless work.

## Structure
- The station predates the Elemental War and was originally used for research on engineered sea creatures, magical ocean currents, and dangerous deep-water life.
- It was abandoned after the war due to staffing collapse, rising maintenance costs, and the danger posed by hostile water elementals in the region.
- Kira did not build the complex; she reclaimed it years later by constructing breathing machines, submersible access tools, and repair systems that let her live below the surface.
- Large sections remain flooded, stripped, or unstable, while only a few zones are fully livable or operational.

## Active Areas
- Docking Bay: A pressure-sealed arrival chamber with Kira's improvised underwater vehicles, breathing gear, and salvage rigs.
- Restored Quarters: Sparse living space, workbenches, journals, and prototype devices assembled from scavenged war wreckage.
- Control Core: A partially restored operations center that manages life support, pressure seals, and whatever remains of the station's old defenses.
- Archive Wing: Locked research stores containing pre-war notes on sea creatures, elemental currents, and void-adjacent anomalies.

## Sustenance and Salvage
- Kira uses hunting drones and underwater machines to harvest sea life for food.
- Filtration systems turn seawater into drinkable water.
- Wrecks from the old wars litter the seabed nearby, providing a steady trickle of usable plating, cores, tools, and scrap.
- Most of the station's original valuables are long gone, but practical salvage and fragments of research still matter.

## Defenses
- Ancient pressure wards and compartment seals still protect key sections from catastrophic flooding.
- Some pre-war security systems still function erratically, especially doors, isolation shutters, and emergency lockdowns.
- Kira has added drones, dampeners, and jury-rigged defenses tuned to disrupt Conclave technology rather than wage open war.
- Distance, secrecy, and the hostility of the surrounding waters remain the lab's greatest protection.

## Services
- Safe refuge from ordinary Conclave surveillance
- Technical interpretation of Conclave engines, portals, and extraction systems
- Equipment repair and modification using salvaged pre-war components
- Limited specialty gear such as breathing devices, pressure-resistant upgrades, scouting drones, and localized essence dampeners if trust is earned

## Secrets
- Kira is trying to stop the Conclave from harvesting the void comet, not to seize it for herself
- The archived research here may contain defensive countermeasures and calibration data more valuable than the station's physical salvage
- The lab is hidden partly because Kira no longer trusts institutions, allies, or even her own legacy without proof
- A small number of old allies, including Orion, may know enough about Kira's older ward logic to recognize traces others would miss
