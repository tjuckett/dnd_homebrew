# Srosa Shops

## Summary
Srosa's shops are small but important anchors of daily life in a dangerous frontier settlement. They provide shelter, equipment, remedies, and routine enough to make the town feel livable despite its borderland tension. Most are practical first and only lightly touched by wonder, with seasonal trinkets and small comforts mattering as much to morale as to trade.

## Shops
- The Hearthstone Haven: Tavern and inn that anchors the settlement's social life, known for hot meals, strong drink, and the stories Cedric Stonehelm can coax out of almost anyone
- Sturdy Steel Smithy: Practical forge focused on tools, repairs, river hardware, and dependable frontier weapons rather than luxury craftsmanship
- Rustic Provisions General Store: Main source of staple goods, lamp oil, winter clothing, writing supplies, and everyday needs that would be hard to replace in an isolated settlement
- Greenleaf Remedies: Small herbal counter supplying winter teas, poultices, dried herbs, and simple tinctures prepared from local cultivation and gathered mountain flora

## Usage Notes
- The shops of Srosa should feel like survival infrastructure with personality rather than a bustling urban market.
- Seasonal stalls in the square may add festival sweets, handmade crafts, and occasional harmless magical curiosities during calmer stretches of winter.
- Prices should generally skew modest and practical unless scarcity, danger, or outside trade disruption becomes a story factor.
