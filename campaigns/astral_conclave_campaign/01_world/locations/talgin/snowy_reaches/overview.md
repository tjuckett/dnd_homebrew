# Snowy Reaches

## Summary
The Snowy Reaches are a cold frontier region of [[Talgin]], marked by rivers, forests, mountain weather, and settlements built to endure isolation. Communities here survive through resilience, caution, and local cooperation rather than any broad regional authority.

## Purpose
The Snowy Reaches provide a regional home for Talgin's colder northern and northwestern settlements, especially those living near old elemental borders and difficult wilderness.

## Tone
Cold, beautiful, and quietly precarious. Life persists here through routine, endurance, and a constant awareness that safety is conditional.
