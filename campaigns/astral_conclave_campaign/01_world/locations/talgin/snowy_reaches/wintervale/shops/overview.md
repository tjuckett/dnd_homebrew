# Wintervale Shops

## Summary
Wintervale's commerce is broader and better supplied than most settlements in the [[Snowy Reaches]]. It supports local survival first, but its market depth also makes it a place where travelers can outfit for harsher journeys north or west.

## Shops
- Emberstone Forge: High-quality weapons, armor, tools, and commissioned work under [[Brenn Ironfist]]
- Northern Bazaar Stalls: Rotating merchants handling ore, timber goods, salvage, winter clothing, preserved food, writing supplies, and occasional arcane curiosities
- Greenthumb's Remedies: Workshop and counter where [[Torel Greenthumb]] sells odd gadgets, alchemical odds and ends, and practical herbal preparations
- Frozen Hearth Inn: Lodging, hot meals, rumor exchange, caravan contact, and seasonal hiring point for guards or guides

## Notes
- Prices should be slightly better than isolated frontier hamlets for ordinary goods, but magical or specialty equipment still depends on scarcity and trust.
- Wintervale can support repairs, custom commissions, and multi-day procurement in a way smaller northern settlements cannot.
