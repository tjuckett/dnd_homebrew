# Wintervale NPCs

## Summary
Wintervale's notable figures are defined less by nobility than by competence. The town depends on people who can keep others fed, equipped, defended, or spiritually steady through long winters.

## Key Figures
- [[Brenn Ironfist]]: Master smith of Emberstone Forge and a respected voice in practical town matters
- [[Ithilwen Moonshadow]]: Head priestess of the Temple of Eternal Winter
- [[Torel Greenthumb]]: Inventor, remedy-maker, and unreliable genius of the Northern Bazaar
