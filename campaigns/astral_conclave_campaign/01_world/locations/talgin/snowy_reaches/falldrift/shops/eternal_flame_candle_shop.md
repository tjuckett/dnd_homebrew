# Eternal Flame Candle Shop

## Summary
The Eternal Flame Candle Shop is a cozy candle-maker's shop in [[Falldrift]], owned by Mayor [[Elara Frostheart]]. Warm firelight, fragrant wax, and carefully crafted displays make it feel more welcoming than commercial, and many locals treat it as both a shop and a quiet place of comfort during the long winter.

## Proprietor
- [[Elara Frostheart]]

## Atmosphere
- A constant warm glow from candles and a welcoming hearth
- Shelves lined with meticulously crafted candles in many sizes and scents
- A soothing, fragrant interior that contrasts with the cold outside

## Goods and Prices
- Everglow Candles: Slow-burning candles with a gentle persistent glow, `2 cp` each
- Frostbloom Aromatics: Scented candles infused with winter flowers and herbs, `5 cp` each
- Enchanted Ember Lanterns: Small lanterns with a magical ever-burning flame, `2 sp` each
- Icicle Sculpted Candles: Decorative candles shaped like icicles, `1 sp` each
- Moonlit Melodies Candle Set: Relaxation-themed candle set with a small music box, `5 sp` per set
- Elemental Essence Oil Burners: Decorative burners releasing fragrant oils tied to local elemental imagery, `3 sp` each
- Candle Carvings Kit: Plain candles and carving tools for custom designs, `1 sp` per kit
- Winter's Embrace Scented Wax Melts: Comforting winter-scented wax packets, `2 cp` per packet

## Special Notes
- Elara sometimes offers limited-edition candles inspired by seasonal shifts or local festivals
- The shop occasionally hosts candle-making workshops where patrons craft personalized candles
- Prices may vary depending on ingredient rarity and the intricacy of the work
