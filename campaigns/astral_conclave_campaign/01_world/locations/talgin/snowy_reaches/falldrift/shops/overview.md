# Falldrift Shops

## Summary
Falldrift's commerce is practical, seasonal, and community-focused. Its shops prioritize warmth, repair, food, and basic comforts over luxury.

## Shops
- Snowflake Provisions: Food staples such as grains, vegetables, and dried meats
- Frostwood Outfitters: Cold-weather clothing, boots, and travel gear
- Ironmug's Smithy: Tools, basic weapons, and armor built from local iron
- Silverstream's Catch: Fresh river fish and preserved seafood
- Threadspinner's Tailoring: Tailored garments, winter wear, and repairs
- Emberforge Potions and Herbs: Healing draughts, remedies, and alchemical supplies
- Snowsong's Trinkets and Oddities: Crafts, curiosities, and unusual imports
- Moonshadow Melodies: Instruments, sheet music, and bardic supplies
- Eternal Flame Candle Shop: Candles, lanterns, and practical light sources
- Frozen Spirits Tavern: Ales, spirits, and hot drinks served around the hearth

## Open Questions
- Bran Ironmug is described as the innkeeper, but one shop list names him as owner of the smithy. Is that a separate family business or a mistaken attribution?
- Thrain Emberforge is presented as the blacksmith, but one shop list ties his name to potions and herbs. Should that shop instead belong to Elyra Frostbloom?
- Greta Frostbloom appears as a shop owner but not in the resident list. Is she a separate villager or an alternate name connected to Elyra?
