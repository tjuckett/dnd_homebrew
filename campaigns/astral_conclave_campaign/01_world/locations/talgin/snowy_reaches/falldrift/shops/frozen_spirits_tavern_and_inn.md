# Frozen Spirits Tavern and Inn

## Summary
The Frozen Spirits Tavern and Inn is a warm and inviting establishment at the heart of [[Falldrift]], run by [[Finn Wildwood]] and [[Bran Ironmug]]. Its sturdy wooden exterior and icicle-shaped decorations promise shelter from the cold, while the roaring fireplace and close-set tables inside make it one of the hamlet's strongest centers of fellowship.

## Proprietors
- [[Finn Wildwood]]
- [[Bran Ironmug]]

## Atmosphere
- Sturdy wooden structure with winter decorations and a welcoming front
- Roaring hearth, wooden tables, and a cozy communal interior
- A gathering place for townsfolk, hunters, and any traveler hardy enough to reach Falldrift
