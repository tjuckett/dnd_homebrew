# Falldrift NPCs

## Summary
Falldrift's residents are the small group of craftspeople, workers, and local fixtures who keep the hamlet alive through long winters.

## Residents
- Mayor Elara Frostheart: Long-serving elven mayor known for diplomacy, compassion, and a strong connection to nature
- Bran Ironmug: Dwarven innkeeper whose Snowflake Inn functions as the social heart of the hamlet
- Mara Silverstream: Half-elf fisher known for working the icy river with confidence and precision
- Finn Wildwood: Human hunter who patrols the outskirts and acts as a practical guardian
- Elyra Frostbloom: Elven herbalist whose winter remedies help the settlement endure the cold and minor dangers
- Thrain Emberforge: Dwarven blacksmith whose forge supports the village's tools, repair work, and defense
- Isabella Snowsong: Tiefling trader bringing uncommon wares and outside news into an isolated place
- Alaric Frostwhisper: Elderly gnome storyteller preserving old tales and giving the hamlet a sense of continuity
- Elowen Threadspinner: Seamstress known for warm garments that suit both practicality and winter beauty
- Lirael Moonshadow: Bard whose music lifts spirits and makes the hamlet feel less cut off from the world
