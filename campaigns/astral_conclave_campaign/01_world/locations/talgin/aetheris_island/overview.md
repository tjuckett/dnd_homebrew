# Aetheris Island

## Summary
[[Aetheris Island]] is an island stronghold within [[Talgin]], used as a major [[Astral Conclave]] headquarters.

## Features
- Walls and watch towers staffed largely by automatons
- Sky dock
- Observatory
- Minimal or no living elemental presence nearby
- [[Northern Base]]
- Security specialists such as [[Vesper "Pinkshade" Arlin]]

## Strategic Use
Acts as a protected staging area for large-scale Conclave research, transport, and command.

## Connected Entities
- [[Talgin]]
- [[Astral Conclave]]
- [[Northern Base]]
- [[Vesper "Pinkshade" Arlin]]
