# Northern Base

## Summary
Aetherion Outpost, commonly referred to as the [[Northern Base]], is a fortified [[Astral Conclave]] settlement positioned under the authority of [[Aetheris Island]]. Established several years ago as a strategic forward base, it has grown into a small town-sized military installation that blends strict discipline, arcane engineering, and limited civilian support infrastructure.

## Purpose
The outpost serves as the Conclave's northern staging ground for transport, surveillance, strategic coordination, and the deployment of advanced devices. It is also a likely destination for experimental power systems and other assets moved out of the south.

## Tone
Disciplined, utilitarian, and quietly imposing. The outpost feels less like a community than a machine built to endure cold, distance, and prolonged conflict.

## Features
- Fortified perimeter of reinforced stone and enchanted metal
- Watch towers staffed by linked automatons with advanced optics and ranged weapons
- A Sky Dock housing airships and astral skimmers for reconnaissance, supply, and rapid deployment
- A cliff-built Observatory with celestial lenses and planar scrying tools, especially focused on tracking the comet
- Experimental heavy-array infrastructure suitable for comet capture, beam projection, or other large-scale astral engineering
- Barracks and training grounds for soldiers, Conclave agents, automaton drills, and magic-enhanced combat exercises
- A dark steel and crystal Command Center containing war rooms and an arcane relay network
- A guarded Supply Depot protected by anti-theft wards

## Defenses
- Elemental-triggered magical defenses
- Circle-based devices storing spells that activate for 1 hour when elementals approach
- Elemental Detection Wards embedded into key perimeter and portal-adjacent approaches
- Small reaction windows during activation cycles
- Strong mechanical and automaton presence
- Arcane shield layers protecting against physical and magical assault
- Automaton patrols across the walls, streets, and perimeter
- Signal relays linked to Aetherbind wrist devices for rapid coordinated response
- Sentinel golems stationed at critical points for last-resort defense

## Strategic Importance
Likely candidate for receiving or integrating humanoid essence-based hearts into larger infrastructure. Its position in the Frostspire Peaks, combined with its aerial capacity and command network, makes it one of the Conclave's most important northern strongholds.

## Population
- Roughly 70% military personnel, artificers, and tactical staff
- A smaller civilian support population of smiths, cooks, engineers, and maintenance workers
- A heavy automaton presence, including labor models, patrol units, and combat constructs

## Atmosphere
The outpost runs on duty, repetition, and readiness. Conclave banners bearing a starburst ringed with arcane runes line the walls, and the northern lights often cast an eerie beauty over an otherwise militarized settlement.

## Secrets
- The observatory is not merely astronomical; it is part of the Conclave's effort to monitor planar and cosmic events tied to the comet.
- The base likely serves as a northern control point for tractor-beam or comet-harvesting infrastructure rather than a passive watch station.
- The scale of the outpost suggests the Conclave intends to hold the north long-term rather than use the site as a temporary encampment.
