# Null Bloom Facility NPCs

## Summary
Bloomwell's known figures fall into three groups: the Conclave leadership running the site, prisoners whose survival carries campaign consequences, and classified subjects whose existence hints at deeper failures inside the facility.

## Conclave Leadership
- [[Director Solenne Varn]]: Augmented director of the facility and chief defender of its extraction program
- [[Inquisitor Taelix Dren]]: Psionic conditioning officer overseeing control implants, interrogations, and ideological discipline

## Prisoners and Survivors
- [[Alden Wren]]: Captive technician who understands the extraction machinery and may know how to break it
- [[Kasha "Whisperclaw"]]: Escaped tabaxi test subject hiding in ducts and maintenance routes
- [[Mira "Silent Step"]]: Psi-resistant tabaxi prisoner kept under sedation in a sealed cell

## Classified Subject
- [[Jinx "Nightwhisker"]]: Subject J-00, a redacted tabaxi captive tied to Vault Omega and the deepest experimental failures
