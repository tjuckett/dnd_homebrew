# Null Bloom Facility

## Summary
Bloomwell is a five-floor underground [[Astral Conclave]] research site hidden in the western reaches of [[The Verdant Expanse]]. Built as a biotech-magitech fusion lab, it was designed to suppress elemental forces, process living captives into usable essence, and test anti-elemental devices far from public scrutiny.

## Purpose
The facility exists to convert living prisoners, stolen planar research, and suppression technology into practical Conclave leverage. It supports humanoid essence extraction, anti-elemental field research, augmentation trials, and controlled experimentation on captives taken through [[Stormhaven]] and nearby routes.

## Tone
Sterile cruelty overgrown by neglect. Bloomwell feels less like a battlefield than a buried machine for turning people into fuel, with clean Conclave geometry laid over panic, rot, and old suffering.

## Floors
1. Experimental Floor: The lowest vault houses the extraction machinery, tractor-beam prototype, containment circles, harvest pods, and essence-routing conduits. Prisoners are brought here to be drained and converted into power for the facility's deeper systems.
2. Logistics Core: Two storage chambers, an automated kitchen, ration processing, and maintenance systems support the lower labs. Hidden service access from this floor may bypass parts of the teleport matrix.
3. Detention Sector: Former residential rooms were converted into prison cells for Stormhaven captives and failed subjects. Suppression locks, control glyphs, and monitoring posts define most of the floor.
4. Residential Wing: Conclave staff quarters, mess hall, recreation space, and small personal workrooms occupy this level. The abrupt abandonment left behind journals, half-used supplies, and signs of internal fear.
5. Portal and Transit Layer: The top floor contains the external portal chamber, security consoles, and the primary teleport room used to move personnel between levels and out of the complex.

## Structure
- The entire complex is buried in worked stone reinforced by geomantic shielding intended to deny intrusion by earth elementals and tunneling magic.
- Standard movement between floors depends on paired teleport rooms keyed to Bloomwell's internal matrix.
- The external portal is tightly controlled and linked to Conclave routes rather than public transportation. Stormhaven's hidden portal infrastructure is one of the most plausible access points into that network.
- If the teleport grid fails, emergency maintenance shafts and service crawlspaces become the only ways to move vertically.

## Security
- Nature-reactive illusion wards conceal the site from casual discovery and complicate magical survey attempts.
- 2 Construct Sentinels and 2 Null Watch Guards are typically assigned to each floor, with 4-6 roaming guards and 10 emergency-response personnel kept in reserve.
- Internal teleportation is heavily permission-locked, making uncontrolled movement through the facility difficult even after entry.
- Suppressive runes, detection wards, and null-field lockdown measures are layered into key corridors, cells, and portal systems.
- Elementals instinctively resist entering the site, and teleportation or summoning magic can become unreliable during security escalation.

## Atrocity
- Innocent people taken from [[Stormhaven]] and surrounding communities are held on the detention floor until selected for transfer downward.
- Prisoners brought to the bottom level are drained through modified extraction equipment, then reduced to husks or used to stabilize further experiments.
- Several humanoid essence-based hearts have already been created and sent north.
- The tractor-beam prototype and related power systems are being fueled in part by energy harvested from captives rather than by a clean arcane source.

## Secrets
- Surviving tabaxi captives still remain inside Bloomwell, including fugitives in the ventilation network and psi-resistant detainees in sealed cells.
- Director Varn's reports understate how unstable the current extraction process has become.
- Subject J-00 was important enough to be erased from routine records and moved under Vault Omega authorization.
- The facility is hardened against direct elemental intrusion, but its dependence on internal teleportation creates exploitable chokepoints if the matrix is compromised.
