# Stormhaven Shops

## Summary
Stormhaven's businesses survive under pressure from the [[Shadow Fangs]], serving sailors, smugglers, mercenaries, and locals trying to outlast the town's decline.

## Notable Shops and Services
- The Rusted Anchor Inn: Run-down dockside tavern and inn run by Marla Vintyr, known for smoky common rooms, secure private meetings, and weary regulars
- Joren's Forge: Blacksmith shop blending traditional ironwork with salvaged pre-war technology; secretly used to support the rebellion
- The Broken Compass: General goods store run by Harlon Greaves, selling mundane supplies, scavenged relics, and goods for trusted customers through a hidden backroom
- The Wreckyard: Salvage ground managed by Torgen Craghewn, where broken ships, carts, and machinery sometimes conceal valuable pre-war scraps
