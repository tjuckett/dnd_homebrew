# Stormhaven NPCs

## Summary
Stormhaven's notable figures are divided between criminal leadership, covert Conclave operators, weary civilians, and a fragile rebellion trying to break the town's dependency. Campaign-significant figures should live in `03_npcs`, while this file stays focused on fast local prep.

## Master NPC Files
- [[Kaelric "The Veil"]]: Shadow Fang ruler of Stormhaven and primary local power broker
- [[Isera Duskfall]]: Conclave liaison overseeing cooperation, secrecy, and portal-linked operations

## Local Supporting Cast
- [[Derrik "Stonehand" Veynor]]: Head enforcer of the Shadow Fangs, fitted with a Conclave-made mechanical arm
- Lysara Greywind: Spy and informant working from the town's edges and hidden tunnels

## Rebellion and Civilian Figures
- [[Eric Torne]]: Ex-mercenary leading Stormhaven's rebellion against Shadow Fang rule
- Lyra Fenwick: Smuggler and spy helping connect the rebellion to outside allies
- Joren Thatch: Blacksmith and resistance supporter, using salvaged pre-war tech to aid the cause
- Marla Vintyr: Hard-bitten proprietor of the Rusted Anchor Inn
- Harlon Greaves: Elderly half-elf merchant with quiet contempt for the Shadow Fangs
- Sister Elira: Caretaker of the Temple of Tides and secret supporter of the rebellion
- Torgen Craghewn: Gruff dwarf scrapper with an eye for valuable salvage
