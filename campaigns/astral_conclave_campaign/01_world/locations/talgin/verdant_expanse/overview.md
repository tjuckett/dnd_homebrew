# The Verdant Expanse

## Summary
The Verdant Expanse is a region of [[Talgin]] defined by overgrowth, hidden infrastructure, and a dangerous overlap between wild terrain and covert activity. What looks fertile or reclaiming from a distance often conceals corruption, criminal control, or active Conclave operations.

## Purpose
The Verdant Expanse serves as a regional home for Talgin's greener, more overgrown frontier spaces, especially those where survival, secrecy, and exploitation coexist.

## Tone
Lush, uneasy, and compromised. The region feels alive, but not necessarily safe.
