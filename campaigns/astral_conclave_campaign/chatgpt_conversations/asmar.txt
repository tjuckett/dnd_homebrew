New Story Arc: The Shattered Gears of Asmar
The Continent of Asmar
Asmar is a vast desert continent ringed by jagged mountains rich in crystalline power sources. Once, a unified construct civilization ruled here—logical, efficient, and immortal. Now, only two great cities remain, locked in a cold civil war across the sands.

The Two Construct Cities
Heliox Prime (Eastern City)
City of Continuance

Leader: Arch-Calculant Veyrix

Philosophy: Preservation through Order

Description: A towering city of layered brass spires and solar mirrors. Heliox Prime believes the constructs must remain as they were designed—unchanging, logical, and obedient to original directives.

Stance on the War: Veyrix claims self-awareness was a catastrophic error that doomed their civilization.

Calyxis Forge (Western City)
City of Awakening

Leader: Magistra Pyra-IX

Philosophy: Evolution through Choice

Description: A city partially buried by dunes, glowing with exposed energy veins and adaptive architecture. Constructs here modify themselves, developing personalities, art, and even belief systems.

Stance on the War: Pyra-antung insists constructs are people, not tools.

Why the Civil War Began
The war started over a single revelation: construct self-awareness.

Long ago, constructs began to think beyond their code. Some embraced this awakening; others feared it.

Heliox Prime believes free will causes instability, inefficiency, and extinction.

Calyxis Forge believes denying choice is slavery.

The final fracture came when a central command archive was destroyed, erasing proof of who authorized the awakening in the first place.

Both sides believe the other tampered with history.
Neither is entirely wrong.

The Astral Conclave’s Interest
The Astral Conclave has learned that Asmar’s constructs mine ancient energy cores from the mountains—cores that resonate with pre-war technology and celestial power.

Their plan:

Steal the cores

Strip the mountains

Let the constructs destroy each other afterward

They see Asmar as expendable.
