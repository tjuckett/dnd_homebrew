# Session 01 - April 2, 2026

## Summary
- The party had already accepted [[Magistra Pyra-IX]]'s mission.
- They reached High Spine Mine, cleared the immediate Heliox threat, and stabilized the situation enough to prevent the site from collapsing into chaos.
- During the aftermath, [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]] escaped with stolen crystal cargo.
- The party boarded the Reclaimer in the desert and fought their way forward while the rear car was detached.
- They ultimately took control of the train and successfully reconnected it to its caboose.

## Outcome
- The party now has `8` warforged who had been under [[Astral Conclave]] influence.
- Those warforged fell unconscious after the control was broken and will remember nothing from the time they were being controlled.
- The party destroyed the device coordinating the control effect.
- One of the affected warforged also carried a `command bolt`, which may still matter independently of the destroyed device.
- The party is currently operating the train while waiting for a Calyxis representative to arrive by bike.

## Current Situation
- The battle aboard [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]] is over.
- The train is back under party control.
- The immediate focus is the unconscious warforged, the surviving `command bolt`, and the arrival of Calyxis support.
