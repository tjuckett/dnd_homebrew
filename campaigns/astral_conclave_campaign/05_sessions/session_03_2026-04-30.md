# Session 03 - April 30, 2026

## Summary
- The party followed the repaired command-bolt detector into the desert aboard [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]].
- They did not fully trust the `20` Calyxis soldiers to remain safe near an unknown command signal.
- Before approaching the source, they detached and left behind `2` of the Reclaimer's cars so the Calyxis personnel would not be standing beside the whole vehicle if the site emitted control signals.
- The party reached the first relay site: [[Sandglass Relay]].
- The site appeared abandoned, scrubbed, and partially purged, with signs that the [[Astral Conclave]] had already evacuated personnel and critical equipment.
- Giant scorpions came up through a broken rock layer beneath the still-warm relay hardware and attacked when the party approached.
- The party used [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]] during the battle at the relay site, which was an important tactical choice that had not been part of the original plan.
- The session ended with the battle against the scorpions resolved or effectively at its end, before the party had completed a full investigation of the site.
