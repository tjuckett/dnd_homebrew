# Session 02 - April 16, 2026

## Summary
- The party had already secured [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]] and the attached train after the previous session's battle.
- They woke two of the surviving warforged who had been under outside control.
- The first survivor was Selix, a Heliox doctor/mechanic. He had no memory of the controlled period and remembered factory or refinement work, likely connected to energy cores.
- The second survivor was Varyn, a female Heliox survivor. She had the command bolt and no memory of the controlled period.
- The party examined the command bolt and the larger control device. They discovered that the larger device broadcast a field that could place local warforged into a stupor where they became suggestible.
- The command bolt was not the source of the stupor field. It carried or transmitted the actual commands to affected warforged.
- A third device was discovered embedded in Varyn's neck. This device would allow the [[Astral Conclave]] to take control of her at any time.
- Varyn destroyed the command bolt, damaging the neck device in the process.
- The discovery of the neck device implied that there may be sleeper agents in [[Calyxis Forge]] or elsewhere who have not yet been activated.

## Outcome
- The party repaired the smashed command bolt enough to repurpose it.
- With Selix's help, they turned the repaired remains into a detector that could trace the command signal.
- The detector pointed toward the center of the desert, but the party initially chose not to pursue the signal.
- Instead, they returned toward the mountains and waited for Calyxis aid.
- The party took a long rest while waiting.
- In the middle of the night, Calyxis help arrived: `20` soldiers and one captain.
- The captain may have been named Talis.
- The party pulled the Calyxis representative and the captain aside to explain the situation.
- With Calyxis support, the party discovered that one of the soldiers had a sleeper-agent control device inside him, though it had not activated yet.
- With Selix's help, they deactivated the device and told the soldier what had been found.
- The party now fears returning to [[Calyxis Forge]] because the [[Astral Conclave]] may know they are there and could activate sleeper agents inside the city.

## Current Situation
- The party has evidence that the [[Astral Conclave]] is using at least three linked control systems:
  - a broadcast device that places nearby warforged into a stupor and makes them suggestible,
  - command bolts that transmit or relay orders,
  - and sleeper-agent devices that allow direct takeover when activated.
- The party knows the signal source is somewhere in the center of the desert.
- The party believes many in [[Heliox Prime]] may be afflicted with sleeper-agent devices or vulnerable to additional stupor-field devices.
- The party also knows that similar devices may exist beyond Heliox, including among Calyxis personnel.
- Rather than returning to [[Calyxis Forge]], the party plans to take the train toward the center of the desert and track down the source of the signal.
