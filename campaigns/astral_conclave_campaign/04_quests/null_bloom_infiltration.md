# Null Bloom Infiltration

## Summary
Enter the Null Bloom Facility, uncover the extent of humanoid essence extraction, and determine whether to sabotage, rescue, or exploit the site.

## Key NPCs
- [[Director Solenne Varn]]
- [[Inquisitor Taelix Dren]]
- [[Alden Wren]]

## Major Choices
- Rescue prisoners or preserve stealth
- Trust Alden Wren with an overload plan
- Destroy evidence, steal evidence, or redirect it toward another faction

## Outcomes
- Public exposure of the Conclave
- Silent but meaningful disruption
- Catastrophic overload with moral cost
