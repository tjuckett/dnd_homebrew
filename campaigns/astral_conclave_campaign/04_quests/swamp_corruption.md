# Swamp Corruption

## Summary
After learning the players can safely remove corruption from an earth elemental, [[Eldara Stonewise]] of [[Stonehearth]] asks the players to investigate [[The Technomire]], a swamp corrupted by ancient technology for about 100 years. The elementals fear it because it causes madness.

## Stakes
The corruption is spreading outward from [[The Technomire]] and encroaching on elemental lands.

## Key Truth
The corruption shares the same energy signature as the corrupted heart, pointing toward a deeper technological source tied to older prototype work and the buried ruins of [[Valtoria]].

## Outcomes
- Cleanse the corruption
- Contain but not solve it
- Discover it is a symptom of a deeper system
