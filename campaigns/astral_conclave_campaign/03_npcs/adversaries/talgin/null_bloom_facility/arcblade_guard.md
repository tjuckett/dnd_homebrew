# Arcblade Guard

## Role
Anti-magic security trooper used by Bloomwell for escort duty, prison suppression, and portal-room defense.

## Profile
Arcblade Guards are disciplined Conclave infantry equipped with null-iron weapons and drilled to interrupt spells before they change the shape of a fight.

## Typical Use
- Guarding teleport rooms and control consoles
- Escorting prisoners between detention and extraction
- Reinforcing chokepoints during a facility breach

## Notes
- They are more precise than brutal, and should feel like a trained response unit rather than common thugs.
- Their gear reflects Conclave access to specialized anti-caster equipment.
