# Null-Blade Enforcer

## Role
Heavy suppression agent deployed when Bloomwell expects magical resistance, organized rescue, or a full prison break.

## Profile
Null-Blade Enforcers are elite Conclave shock troops wrapped in null-reactive armor and trained to crush concentration, teleportation, and morale in the same motion.

## Typical Use
- Leading emergency response teams
- Holding the detention floor during extraction cycles
- Guarding high-value labs and unstable subjects

## Notes
- Their presence should signal that the facility has moved from routine control to active lockdown.
- They work best as corridor anchors backed by lighter guards or constructs.
