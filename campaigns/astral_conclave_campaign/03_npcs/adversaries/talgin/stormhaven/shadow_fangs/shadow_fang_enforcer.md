# Shadow Fang Enforcer

## Role
Standard occupation enforcer used by the [[Shadow Fangs]] in [[Stormhaven]].

## Profile
These enforcers are the visible face of Shadow Fang rule: aggressive, mobile, and equipped just well enough with Conclave-adjacent tricks to keep common townsfolk terrified.

## Typical Use
- Market patrols
- Dockside intimidation
- Tax collection
- Rebel suppression in lower town alleys

## Notes
- Shadow Step and brutal stun pressure make them dangerous in cramped streets and dim interiors.
- They work best in teams rather than as solo duelists.
