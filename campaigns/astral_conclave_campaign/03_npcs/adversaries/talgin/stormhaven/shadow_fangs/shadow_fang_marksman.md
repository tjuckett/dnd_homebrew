# Shadow Fang Marksman

## Role
Long-range Shadow Fang agent used for rooftop control, ambush support, and perimeter overwatch.

## Profile
Marksmen are selected for patience, mobility, and a willingness to kill from concealment. They are especially useful in Stormhaven's upper walkways, warehouses, and ruined facades.

## Typical Use
- Covering dockside operations
- Watching manor approaches
- Picking off healers or spellcasters during urban fights

## Notes
- They are strongest in dim light, where stealth and extra necrotic pressure combine.
- Use them sparingly in open streets and more often in layered encounters with melee support.
