# Shadow Fang Elite Guard

## Role
Heavy personal guard and elite defender used by the [[Shadow Fangs]] for their most sensitive operations.

## Profile
Elite Guards are the best-equipped human enforcers in Stormhaven, carrying Conclave-supplied weapons and shield tech that regular street patrols do not receive.

## Typical Use
- Guarding Blackthorn Manor
- Escorting Kaelric or other lieutenants
- Reinforcing hidden auctions, labs, or portal access routes

## Notes
- This block came from a late, more combat-focused section of the conversation but still fits stable Stormhaven occupation forces.
- Their equipment is notably stronger than the baseline enforcer kit and should feel rare in play.
