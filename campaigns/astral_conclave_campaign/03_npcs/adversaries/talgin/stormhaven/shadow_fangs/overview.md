# Shadow Fangs Adversaries

## Summary
These are reusable Stormhaven combatants tied to the [[Shadow Fangs]] and their Conclave-supplied equipment. They are the practical muscle of occupation: patrol units, snipers, and elite enforcers built to intimidate, suppress, and hold chokepoints.

## Roles
- Shadow Fang Enforcer: Standard intimidation and patrol bruiser
- Shadow Fang Marksman: Long-range pressure and rooftop control
- Shadow Fang Elite Guard: Heavy personal guard and manor-defense veteran

## Usage Notes
- Use these units for street patrols, dockside security, manor invasions, and rebellion crackdowns.
- They are generic combatants, not named story figures by default.
- Their gear should feel like stolen or supplied pre-war tech rather than polished military issue.
