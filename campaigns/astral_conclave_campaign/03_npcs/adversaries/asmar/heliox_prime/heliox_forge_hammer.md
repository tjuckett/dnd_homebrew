# Heliox Forge-Hammer

## Role
Standard frontline Heliox trooper equipped for close-range restraint and heavy labor-combat crossover duty.

## Profile
Forge-Hammers are practical line units: sturdy, heat-resistant, and built to pin down targets while more specialized Heliox units complete mission-critical objectives.

## Typical Use
- Guarding sabotage engineers
- Locking down narrow platforms and catwalks
- Acting as the dependable backbone of a Heliox strike group

## Notes
- This is the easiest Heliox unit to reuse at scale in patrols and mine encounters.
