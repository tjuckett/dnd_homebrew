# Heliox Engine Overseer

## Role
Senior construct crew officer responsible for engine integrity, steering response, and combat overclock protocols aboard Heliox mobile siege platforms.

## Profile
Engine Overseers combine technical authority with battlefield resilience. They are less flashy than Heliox elites, but far more important to any moving war machine staying alive under pressure.

## Typical Use
- Maintaining or overclocking a crystal engine during combat
- Coordinating steering shifts and subsystem survival
- Defending engine rooms and internal command compartments

## Notes
- Use these constructs when you want players to feel that disabling the machine matters as much as defeating its guards.
- They make excellent priority targets in boarding encounters.
