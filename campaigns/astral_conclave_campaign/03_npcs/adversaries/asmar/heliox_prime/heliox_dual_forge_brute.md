# Heliox Dual-Forge Brute

## Role
Large breakthrough construct and heavy infrastructure-clearing unit used by [[Heliox Prime]].

## Profile
Dual-Forge Brutes are large-bodied Heliox enforcers armed with paired elemental hammers. They are built for brutal area denial, forced repositioning, and terror through overwhelming controlled impact.

## Typical Use
- Breaking fortified mine entrances
- Holding chokepoints against multiple defenders
- Acting as the centerpiece threat in a sabotage team

## Notes
- The frost/cinder split makes them memorable and helps signal Heliox’s “calculated force” doctrine rather than random berserker violence.
