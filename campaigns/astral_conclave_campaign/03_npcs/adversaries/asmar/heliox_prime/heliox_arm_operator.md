# Heliox Arm Operator

## Role
Heavy control-station construct assigned to the mobile fortress claws and close-range boarding rejection.

## Profile
Arm Operators are robust Heliox crew units built to hold their ground at mechanical control stations, manipulate industrial-strength grippers, and physically remove intruders from vulnerable vehicle surfaces.

## Typical Use
- Operating the claw stations of a mobile fortress
- Throwing riders, boarders, or debris off exterior hulls
- Holding chokepoints near front-car control systems

## Notes
- These units are ideal for chase-to-boarding encounters where Heliox wants to deny access rather than simply kill at range.
- They should feel like practical siege crew, not elegant duelists.
