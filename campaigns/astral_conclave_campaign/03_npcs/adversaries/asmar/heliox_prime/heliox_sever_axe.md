# Heliox Sever-Axe

## Role
Shock skirmisher and pursuit unit used by [[Heliox Prime]].

## Profile
Sever-Axes are disciplined assault frames designed to close distance fast, hit hard, and break lines before opponents can stabilize.

## Typical Use
- Chasing fleeing defenders
- Knocking targets prone near ledges, rails, or extraction machinery
- Supporting sabotage cells with direct frontline pressure

## Notes
- These are good “first Heliox melee unit” opponents because they feel forceful without overshadowing elites.
