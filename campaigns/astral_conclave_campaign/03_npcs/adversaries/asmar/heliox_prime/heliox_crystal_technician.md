# Heliox Crystal Technician

## Role
Utility construct assigned to crystal cargo, volatile transport systems, and sabotage-sensitive freight compartments.

## Profile
Crystal Technicians are not front-line soldiers by design, but they are dangerous in close quarters because their work gear, cargo tools, and fail-state detonations turn every cargo bay into a hazard.

## Typical Use
- Guarding or moving seized crystal stores
- Defending refinery cars and containment rooms
- Sabotaging catwalks, latches, and boarding routes

## Notes
- These are good pressure units for the middle phase of a vehicle heist encounter.
- Their deaths should make cargo areas feel unstable and risky.
