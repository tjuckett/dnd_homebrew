# Heliox Harpoon Gunner

## Role
Roof-mounted precision construct responsible for anti-vehicle fire, tethering targets, and controlling the fortress harpoon system.

## Profile
Harpoon Gunners stabilize themselves against violent movement and specialize in pinning fast-moving threats long enough for Heliox heavies to finish the job.

## Typical Use
- Disabling desert skimmers and convoy vehicles
- Pulling boarders off safe approach lines
- Operating roof harpoon stations during fortress pursuit

## Notes
- These units help Heliox feel tactically severe rather than recklessly destructive.
- They are strongest when backed by hull control, terrain pressure, or forced movement.
