# Heliox Prime Adversaries

## Summary
These are reusable Heliox combat units extracted from the Asmar design notes. They represent preservationist battlefield doctrine: suppression, infrastructure denial, disciplined force, and a willingness to cripple assets in order to save the larger system.

## Roles
- Heliox Runebinder: Arcane control and anti-magic support
- Heliox Sever-Axe: Fast pressure and knockdown skirmisher
- Heliox Dual-Forge Brute: Large shock unit built around elemental hammer impact
- Heliox Forge-Hammer: Standard frontline hammer trooper with restraint capability
- Heliox Arm Operator: Station brute for claw controls and boarding denial
- Heliox Harpoon Gunner: Roof-mounted anti-vehicle precision unit
- Heliox Engine Overseer: Command construct for fortress engines and subsystem integrity
- Heliox Crystal Technician: Volatile cargo specialist and compartment defender
- Heliox Security Construct: Heavy hatch and passage blocker
- The Calyxis Reclaimer: Mobile siege fortress used in desert crystal-heist raids

## Usage Notes
- These units are suitable for Heliox sabotage teams, mine raids, checkpoint fights, and cold-war escalation encounters.
- They are generic combatants, not individually important NPCs by default.
- If one of them becomes a named recurring rival, promote that individual into `03_npcs/major` or `03_npcs/minor`.
