# The Calyxis Reclaimer

## Role
Heliox mobile fortress platform used for crystal seizure, convoy interdiction, and infrastructure-denial raids across the Asmar desert.

## Profile
The Calyxis Reclaimer is a three-car siege crawler mounted on immense tank treads and built for hostile extraction operations. Its front drill can break through mine walls and barricades, its middle car secures stolen crystal freight, and its internal compartments let Heliox fight while remaining in motion.

## Typical Use
- Seizing crystal shipments from Calyxis mine routes
- Launching mobile raids beyond fixed Heliox territory
- Forcing boarding fights under moving battlefield conditions

## Notes
- The Reclaimer is best used as a set-piece rather than a routine combatant.
- Its crewed systems matter: claws, harpoons, and steering should feel easier to disrupt by attacking operators or subsystems rather than only by burning through total hit points.
