# Heliox Security Construct

## Role
Heavy boarding-denial unit used to hold doors, hatches, and narrow internal passages on Heliox vehicles and fortified assets.

## Profile
Security Constructs are mobile shield-walls built to keep enemies from advancing through tight spaces. They excel in compartment fights, catwalk defense, and forcing intruders back into environmental danger.

## Typical Use
- Guarding doors and roof-access hatches
- Holding catwalks during boarding actions
- Protecting engine rooms, command cells, and crystal freight

## Notes
- These make strong “you are now inside the machine” enemies because they turn cramped interiors into real tactical problems.
- They pair well with technicians or gunners rather than operating alone.
