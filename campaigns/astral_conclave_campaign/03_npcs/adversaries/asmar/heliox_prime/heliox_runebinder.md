# Heliox Runebinder

## Role
Arcane suppression unit and battlefield controller used by [[Heliox Prime]].

## Profile
Runebinders are preservationist war-casters built to restrain enemies, counter hostile spellwork, and shut down unstable escalation before it becomes impossible to contain.

## Typical Use
- Escorting sabotage teams
- Locking down mine entrances and transit chokepoints
- Countering Calyxis engineers, mages, and unstable devices

## Notes
- Runebinders are ideal for showing that Heliox favors controlled disabling force over chaotic slaughter.
- Their magic reads as geometric, cold, and procedural rather than mystical.
