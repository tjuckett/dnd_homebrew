# Astral Conclave - Asmar Field Units

## Summary
These [[Astral Conclave]] units support temporary relay operations, obedience-device telemetry, and extraction logistics on [[Asmar]].

## Units
- [[Astral Conclave Field Technician]]: Light technical crew for relay teardown, purge crystals, and portal assistance.
- [[Astral Conclave Relay Handler]]: Field commander and emergency portal operator.
- [[Astral Conclave Veil Escort]]: Light escort that screens retreats and buys time.

## Use Notes
- These units should usually try to escape rather than fight to the death.
- Their battlefield goal is evidence denial and portal extraction.
- They fit the Sandglass Relay encounter after the capture of [[The Calyxis Reclaimer]].
