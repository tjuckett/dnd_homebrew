# Astral Conclave Veil Escort

## Role
Light bodyguard and extraction escort assigned to protect Conclave technicians long enough for them to escape.

## Profile
Veil Escorts are not heavy soldiers. They specialize in smoke, misdirection, and holding a line for one or two rounds while more important agents vanish.

## Typical Use
- Covering portal retreats
- Breaking pursuit through smoke and forced movement
- Guarding temporary field sites
- Dying last only if the mission demands it

## Sandglass Relay Tactics
- Starts between the party and the Relay Handler.
- Uses Veil Screen early.
- Shoves, blinds, or slows targets rather than fighting to the death.
