# Astral Conclave Field Technician

## Role
Mobile relay crew, evidence scrubber, and crystal-signal technician used by the [[Astral Conclave]] during temporary field operations on [[Asmar]].

## Profile
Field Technicians are not front-line killers. They are dangerous because they know what to break, burn, pack, or purge before anyone can prove what the Conclave was doing.

## Typical Use
- Packing relay equipment while guards stall intruders
- Burning notes, command slates, and signal rubbings
- Maintaining temporary portals and crystal batteries
- Fleeing with the most incriminating component

## Sandglass Relay Tactics
- First priority is evidence denial.
- Uses Purge Crystal on command logs or relay notes.
- Assists the Relay Handler's portal stabilization if escape is possible.
- Fights only long enough to run.
