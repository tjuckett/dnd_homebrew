# Adversary Organization

## Purpose
`03_npcs/adversaries` stores reusable hostile units, patrol types, elite faction operatives, and other combatants that need to stay easy to find without overloading the campaign-significant NPC folders.

## Structure
- Organize first by region, arc, or continent.
- Organize second by faction, city, or creature family.
- Keep each combatant as a short lore `.md` file paired with an adjacent `.statblock.json` file when a 5eTools block exists.

## Use This Folder For
- Enemy squads and battlefield roles likely to recur
- Hostile faction units that are more important tactically than personally
- Imported or extracted statblocks from design chats that should not stay buried in conversation logs

## Promote Out of Here When
- A specific adversary becomes a named recurring person with goals, relationships, and campaign consequences
- The narrative importance of the individual matters more than the reusable unit template
