# Kaelric "The Veil"

## Role
Shadow Fang ruler of [[Stormhaven]] and the criminal broker maintaining the town's arrangement with the [[Astral Conclave]].

## Description
Kaelric is a half-elf with sharp green eyes, a scar along his jaw, and the unnerving composure of someone who rarely raises his voice because he rarely needs to.

## Goals
- Preserve Shadow Fang control over [[Stormhaven]] without pushing the town into open revolt
- Profit from smuggling, extortion, and covert access to the hidden portal beneath town
- Keep the Conclave useful enough to empower him, but not strong enough to casually discard him

## Personality
Measured, coldly pragmatic, and theatrical only when intimidation serves a purpose. Kaelric prefers leverage, secrets, and dependency over slaughter.

## Secrets
- He knows the Conclave's presence in Stormhaven is more dangerous than most of his own lieutenants understand
- He fears the town may become expendable if the portal caverns spiral into a containment failure

## Hooks
- Can be approached as a tyrant to overthrow, a broker to manipulate, or a local power whose survival may briefly align with the players' goals
- May trade information on Conclave movements if doing so protects his own position
- Plans Shadow Fang operations from a grounded but well-defended war room, where selected leaders keep Conclave-supplied magical equipment for crises and high-risk missions
