# Magistra Pyra-IX

## Role
Ruler of [[Calyxis Forge]] and chief advocate of the Continuance Doctrine on [[Asmar]].

## Goals
- Keep Calyxis Forge operational long enough to outlive the current energy decline
- Defend mountain crystal extraction without openly breaking the truce with [[Heliox Prime]]
- Determine whether the construct player character is truly the Sovereign of Accord and what that means for the [[Elemental Crucible]]
- Prevent the [[Astral Conclave]] from turning Asmar's crisis into an extraction colony

## Personality
Measured, incisive, and convincing without theatrics. Pyra-IX is not sentimental, but she is genuinely committed to continuity and does not confuse caution with passivity.

## Secrets
- She suspects the Crucible is obeying hidden safeguards rather than simply collapsing, but she cannot prove it
- The construct PC's return unsettles her because it reintroduces unfinished intent into a system Calyxis has learned to survive without
- If forced to choose between the truce and Calyxis survival, she will break the truce

## Hooks
- She is the first major Asmar authority likely to believe the party's warning about the [[Astral Conclave]]
- Pyra-IX can hire the party to guard the High Spine Mine as neutral outsiders
- She becomes a powerful ally or a dangerous reformer depending on how the party handles the Crucible and the Sovereign reveal
