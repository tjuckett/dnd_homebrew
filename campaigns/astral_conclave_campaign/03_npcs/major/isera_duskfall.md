# Isera Duskfall

## Role
[[Astral Conclave]] liaison embedded in [[Stormhaven]] to supervise local cooperation, portal access, and field experimentation.

## Goals
- Maintain smooth cooperation between the Conclave and the [[Shadow Fangs]]
- Protect the secrecy and utility of the portal caverns beneath [[Stormhaven]]
- Expand Stormhaven's value as a discreet proving ground for augmentations and controlled deployments

## Personality
Polished, observant, and quietly superior. Isera presents herself as calm and reasonable even when arranging coercive outcomes.

## Secrets
- She evaluates Stormhaven not as a community to stabilize, but as a test environment whose people can be measured, pressured, and replaced
- She keeps contingency plans for removing Shadow Fang assets if they become liabilities

## Hooks
- A useful face for negotiation scenes where the Conclave appears civilized while hiding predatory intent
- A strong source of intelligence on regional logistics if pressured, deceived, or turned against her superiors

## Connections
- [[Stormhaven]]
- [[Astral Conclave]]
- [[Shadow Fangs]]
