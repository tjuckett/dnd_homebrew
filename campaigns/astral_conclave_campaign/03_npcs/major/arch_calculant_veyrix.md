# Arch-Calculant Veyrix

## Role
Leader of [[Heliox Prime]] and foremost defender of the Preservation Doctrine on [[Asmar]].

## Goals
- Reduce extraction rates until Asmar's remaining crystal future can be mathematically sustained
- Contain or cripple Calyxis expansion before it becomes irreversible
- Preserve the cold truce where possible, but violate it covertly if necessary to stop greater depletion
- Control knowledge about the Sovereign of Accord's possible return before it destabilizes both cities

## Personality
Cold, exacting, and profoundly sincere. Veyrix believes mercy without limits is just another word for denial.

## Secrets
- He likely possesses partial records confirming the construct PC's pre-partition executive status
- He supports sabotage operations against Calyxis mining infrastructure while maintaining public truce posture
- He fears the [[Astral Conclave]], but also fears that desperation will make Calyxis vulnerable to outside bargains

## Hooks
- A future meeting with Veyrix can reframe Heliox from faceless saboteurs into a city acting from terrible math
- His archives may contain some of the clearest surviving records about the Sovereign's departure
- If the party proves the wider ley crisis is real, Veyrix may become a hard but rational ally
