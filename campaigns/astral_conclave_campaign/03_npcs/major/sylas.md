# Sylas

## Role
Agent of fate focused on preserving world balance.

## Motivation
He sees a great calamity approaching and believes the players are entangled in the threads leading south. He is not yet certain whether they will prevent the disaster or cause it.

## Traits
Calm, watchful, indirect, unsettlingly patient.

## Companion
A familiar owl that can guide others to him.

## Connections
- Helped [[Orion]]
- Watches the players from a distance
