# Director Solenne Varn

## Role
Director of the [[Null Bloom Facility]] and chief architect of Bloomwell's living extraction program.

## Goals
- Increase extraction efficiency without losing control of the facility
- Prove that augmentation and essence conversion can outperform unstable conventional spellcraft
- Prevent captives, staff, or rivals from exposing how fragile Bloomwell's systems have become

## Personality
Clinical, exacting, and emotionally distant. Varn treats human cost as a variable to manage, but she becomes visibly sharper when her competence or control is questioned.

## Secrets
- Varn relies on heavy magitech augmentation rather than spellcasting, making her both more resilient and more dependent on Bloomwell's infrastructure than most Conclave directors
- She knows the current extraction model is materially less stable than official reports claim
- Subject J-00's redaction was not routine damage control; Varn considers that file dangerous even by Conclave standards

## Hooks
- A strong final authority figure for a Bloomwell incursion whose confidence can crack under escalating systems failure
- A useful source of hard intelligence on how the Conclave is turning living essence into practical infrastructure
