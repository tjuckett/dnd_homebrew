# Kira

## Role
Former [[Astral Conclave]] artificer whose research shaped planar breach technology, elemental tuning rods, and several later systems the Conclave now abuses.

## Goals
- Prevent the [[Astral Conclave]] from harvesting the void comet at full scale
- Stop any plan that would erase elemental life from the world
- Decipher surviving pre-war research that might expose weaknesses in Conclave engines and extraction arrays

## Personality
Brilliant, defensive, and exhausted by isolation. Kira is sharp-tongued when cornered, but beneath the paranoia she still wants to prevent catastrophe more than she wants revenge.

## Secrets
- She fled the Conclave after realizing its leadership was willing to pursue elemental genocide rather than controlled containment
- She is hiding in a decommissioned underwater research station off the western coast of the [[Forbidden Lands]]
- Parts of her earlier work contributed to the current crisis, which leaves her torn between guilt, urgency, and fear that no one should trust her

## Hooks
- A difficult but potentially invaluable ally who can explain how Conclave devices actually work
- A source of tuning rod calibration notes, anti-Conclave sabotage options, and specialized survival gear
- If trust is broken, she can seal her refuge, vanish into the ocean, or become a dangerous loose end with too much knowledge
