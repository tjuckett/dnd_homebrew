# Inquisitor Taelix Dren

## Role
Conclave inquisitor and psionic conditioning officer assigned to the [[Null Bloom Facility]].

## Goals
- Break resistance among prisoners, defectors, and compromised staff
- Perfect control implants and psi-conditioning methods for future Conclave use
- Prove that mercy, hesitation, and divided loyalty are operational failures

## Personality
Manipulative, zealous, and fascinated by minds that do not break the way they should. Taelix frames cruelty as discipline and treats every resistant prisoner as a puzzle to solve.

## Secrets
- His work with tabaxi prisoners has become personally obsessive, especially around psi-resistant subjects and the erased J-00 file
- Repeated exposure to Bloomwell's experiments may be causing subtle psionic bleed and worsening instability

## Hooks
- A strong interrogator-villain for prison scenes, captures, and moral pressure
- His notes can reveal which prisoners matter most to the Conclave and why
