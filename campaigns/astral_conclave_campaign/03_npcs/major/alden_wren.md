# Alden Wren

## Role
Captive technician with intimate knowledge of Bloomwell's modified extraction device and internal transit systems.

## Goals
- Survive long enough to stop the extraction device from claiming more prisoners
- Preserve whatever evidence remains of the Conclave's crimes
- Ensure his family's deaths are not reduced to a successful trial run

## Personality
Exhausted, grief-stricken, and intensely practical. Alden is not reckless by nature, but despair has made him willing to consider sacrifices he would once have rejected.

## Secrets
- His family has already been consumed by the device he once helped maintain
- He knows how to overload and destroy the modified containment device, but doing so may require self-sacrifice
- He understands enough of Bloomwell's internal teleport permissions to help bypass or sabotage movement controls if given time

## Hooks
- Rescue target, tragic ally, or last-resort saboteur
- A natural source of floor-by-floor intelligence if the party reaches him before Varn or Taelix does
