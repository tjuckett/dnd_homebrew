# Jinx "Nightwhisker"

## Role
Redacted tabaxi captive known in Bloomwell records as Subject J-00.

## Goals
- Unknown; surviving records are too heavily censored to trust at face value

## Personality
Unknown. Witness accounts imply fragmented speech, volatile instincts, and flashes of deliberate intelligence after the lower-lab breach.

## Secrets
- Jinx was transferred under Vault Omega authorization and then effectively erased from ordinary facility records
- Evidence suggests Jinx either escaped containment or was moved during a crisis that the Conclave never fully contained
- Both Varn and Taelix treat J-00 as more dangerous than an ordinary failed experiment

## Hooks
- A missing-person mystery inside Bloomwell that can turn into an ally, horror reveal, or unstable third force
- A clue trail connecting redacted paperwork, broken containment, and the facility's deepest systems
