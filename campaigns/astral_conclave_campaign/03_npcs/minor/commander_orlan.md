# Commander Orlan

## Role
Military commander of [[Northern Base]] / Aetherion Outpost.

## Goals
- Keep the outpost secure and operational in the Frostspire Peaks
- Maintain reliable coordination with the wider [[Astral Conclave]]
- Balance direct Conclave orders against the practical needs of the settlement under his command

## Personality
Pragmatic, disciplined, and steady under pressure. Orlan values results over ideology, but his loyalty to the Conclave remains firm.

## Secrets
He likely understands that survival at the outpost sometimes requires bending doctrine, even when official policy leaves little room for it.

## Hooks
- Could serve as a hard but reasonable military obstacle rather than a fanatic
- May be pressured by failures in supply, communication, or experimental systems arriving from the south

## Connections
- [[Northern Base]]
- [[Astral Conclave]]
