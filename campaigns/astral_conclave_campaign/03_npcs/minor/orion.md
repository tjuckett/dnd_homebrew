# Orion

## Role
Wandering star-guided druid and potential ally against the [[Astral Conclave]]'s comet-harvesting plans.

## Goals
- Prevent the Conclave from successfully harvesting or stabilizing the void comet
- Preserve cosmic and planar balance rather than let comet power become another industrial weapon
- Reach or aid [[Kira]] if her hidden research can still help prevent catastrophe

## Personality
Reflective, cautious, and sincere. Orion comes across as gentle at first, but he becomes unexpectedly forceful when someone treats cosmic imbalance as an acceptable cost.

## Hooks
- May know enough about Kira's older wards and concealment methods to help others locate her refuge
- Could be encountered as a prisoner, fugitive, or covert saboteur tied to Conclave tractor-beam infrastructure
- Can offer strategic insight into comet timing, ward gaps, and the larger danger of misusing astral power
