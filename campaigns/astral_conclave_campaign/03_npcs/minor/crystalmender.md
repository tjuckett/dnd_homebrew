# Crystalmender

## Role
Healer and ritualist of [[Stonehearth]], responsible for restorative rites, healing springs, and spiritual care.

## Goals
- Preserve the health and spiritual balance of the village
- Maintain the Healing Springs and crystal-based rites
- Counter corruption and elemental instability where possible

## Personality
Gentle, patient, and attentive. Crystalmender carries quiet authority rooted in care rather than command.

## Secrets
His healing knowledge may reveal early signs of corruption before warriors or leaders recognize the danger.

## Hooks
- Can offer healing, purification rituals, or insight into elemental afflictions
- May need help gathering rare materials or protecting sacred sites tied to recovery work

## Connections
- [[Stonehearth]]
- [[Elemental Corruption]]
