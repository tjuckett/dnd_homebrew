# Mira "Silent Step"

## Role
Psi-resistant tabaxi prisoner held in sealed detention at the [[Null Bloom Facility]].

## Goals
- Survive interrogation and retain her sense of self
- Resist Conclave conditioning long enough to be freed or recover
- Protect what she knows about the facility's deeper experiments

## Personality
Withdrawn, sharp, and difficult to read. Mira's silence can look like collapse, but much of it is deliberate refusal.

## Secrets
- Conclave officers cannot reliably impose mental control on her, which makes her unusually valuable to Taelix
- Sedation has kept her alive, but not fully broken; the right intervention could bring back a capable ally

## Hooks
- A rescue target whose survival directly undermines Taelix's research claims
- A possible source of insight into psi-conditioning, prisoner routines, and the behavior of other tabaxi captives
