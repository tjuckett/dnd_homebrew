# Chief Engineer Eryndor

## Role
Head of the Observatory at [[Northern Base]] and overseer of key arcane technologies stationed there.

## Goals
- Track the comet and other cosmic irregularities
- Maintain the outpost's observatory systems, relays, and specialized arcane devices
- Translate celestial and planar data into usable intelligence for the [[Astral Conclave]]

## Personality
Methodical, absorbed, and intellectually driven. Eryndor is more interested in patterns and systems than in battlefield glory.

## Secrets
His work likely reveals more about the comet and the wider planar situation than most Conclave personnel are permitted to know.

## Hooks
- Could become a vital source of technical information if pressured, persuaded, or disillusioned
- May prioritize preserving observatory data over preserving lives during a crisis

## Connections
- [[Northern Base]]
- [[Astral Conclave]]
