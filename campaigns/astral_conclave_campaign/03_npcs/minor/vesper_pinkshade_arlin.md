# Vesper "Pinkshade" Arlin

## Role
High-trust [[Astral Conclave]] operative assigned to security, infiltration, and internal cleanup around [[Aetheris Island]].

## Personality
Cool, taunting, and ruthlessly pragmatic. Vesper believes in the Conclave's mission enough to do ugly work for it, but she is not suicidally loyal and will retreat if survival serves the assignment.

## Appearance
A sharp-featured half-elf with bright pink hair, fitted dark armor, and movements precise enough to feel rehearsed. She cultivates a memorable look on purpose and uses it to make others underestimate her.

## Function
- Oversees sensitive security around comet-harvesting infrastructure
- Handles infiltration, pursuit, and recovery of dangerous loose ends
- Serves as the kind of Conclave opponent who can escape, regroup, and return smarter

## Equipment
- Favors a venom-treated dagger, stealth magic, and mobility tools over direct battlefield dominance
- Carries an ancient Elemental Suppression Bracer, a pre-war wrist device designed to hinder rogue elementals and interface only with more sophisticated sync hardware than most surviving portal tools can provide
