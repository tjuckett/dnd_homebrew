# Eric Torne

## Role
Former mercenary leading the rebellion against [[Shadow Fangs]] control in [[Stormhaven]].

## Goals
- Break Shadow Fang rule and restore Stormhaven's independence
- Protect the rebellion long enough to survive betrayal, scarcity, and retaliation
- Expose or destroy the [[Astral Conclave]]'s local experimentation before it consumes the town

## Personality
Grizzled, determined, and more disciplined than hopeful. Eric still thinks like a veteran organizer, even when grief pushes him toward reckless choices.

## Secrets
- His losses under Shadow Fang rule make him vulnerable to vengeance-driven mistakes
- He suspects the killings and disappearances around Stormhaven connect to something larger than ordinary criminal cruelty, but he lacks proof

## Hooks
- A natural contact for players willing to work with the rebellion
- His network can provide safehouses, local intelligence, and sabotage leads if trust is earned

## Connections
- [[Stormhaven]]
- [[Shadow Fangs]]
- [[Astral Conclave]]
