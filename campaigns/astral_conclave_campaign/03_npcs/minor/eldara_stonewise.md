# Eldara Stonewise

## Role
High chieftain of [[Stonehearth]] and primary leader of its earth elemental community.

## Goals
- Preserve the long-term safety and continuity of [[Stonehearth]]
- Guide the village through threats without sacrificing its traditions
- Seek trustworthy allies against spreading corruption and other destabilizing forces

## Personality
Ancient, deliberate, and wise. Eldara carries the authority of someone who has endured many eras and prefers measured action over reckless force.

## Secrets
She likely understands that Stonehearth cannot remain entirely isolated if corruption continues to spread into elemental lands.

## Hooks
- Can serve as a diplomatic patron or cautious ally for the players
- May ask outsiders to investigate threats earth elementals cannot ignore but do not fully understand

## Connections
- [[Stonehearth]]
- [[Swamp Corruption]]
