# Brenn Ironfist

## Role
Master smith of [[Wintervale]]'s Emberstone Forge.

## Personality
Gruff, exacting, disciplined, and far kinder than he first appears. Brenn respects work ethic, patience, and anyone who treats a craft as something worth doing properly.

## Appearance
Broad-shouldered dwarf with a soot-dark beard streaked in silver, scarred forearms, and heavy smith's braids marked by iron rings of clan significance.

## Background
Brenn comes from an old dwarven smithing line broken by disaster long ago. After years of wandering, he settled in [[Wintervale]], rebuilt a decaying forge into a regional institution, and made teaching part of how he honors what his family lost.

## Craft and Teaching
- Oversees most serious forge work personally
- Trains only a small number of apprentices at a time
- Starts students on discipline, repetition, and tool care before allowing any ambitious projects
- Accepts new apprentices only after a practical trial and a longer test of endurance, humility, and consistency

## Traits
- Mutters through forging steps under his breath
- Struggles to delegate delicate work
- Quietly takes pride in apprentices who outgrow his first expectations

## Connections
- [[Wintervale]]
