# Torel Greenthumb

## Role
Gnome inventor in Wintervale's Northern Bazaar.

## Traits
Brilliant, scatterbrained, unreliable in execution.

## Specialty
Mechanical and enchanted gadgets, often built from salvaged warforged parts.

## Notes
Despite his erratic focus, Torel remains one of [[Wintervale]]'s most useful practical minds whenever damaged devices, improvised fixes, or strange salvage are involved.
