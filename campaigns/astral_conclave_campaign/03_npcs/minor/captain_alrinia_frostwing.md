# Captain Alrinia Frostwing

## Role
Commander of the Sky Dock and aerial operations at [[Northern Base]].

## Goals
- Keep aerial reconnaissance and supply lines functional despite the mountain conditions
- Protect the outpost's airships and astral skimmers from sabotage or interception
- Use mobility and aggressive positioning to keep the Conclave ahead of emerging threats

## Personality
Bold, sharp-minded, and action-oriented. Alrinia carries herself like a veteran adventurer who learned to turn risk into advantage.

## Secrets
Her reputation for daring tactics may hide a willingness to take operational risks that more cautious commanders would reject.

## Hooks
- A strong candidate for aerial pursuit, interception, or tense negotiation scenes
- May respect capable opponents more than political superiors

- [[Northern Base]]
- [[Astral Conclave]]
