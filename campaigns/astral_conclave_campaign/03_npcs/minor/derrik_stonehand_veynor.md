# Derrik "Stonehand" Veynor

## Role
Head enforcer of the [[Shadow Fangs]] in [[Stormhaven]] and chief battlefield lieutenant to [[Kaelric "The Veil"]].

## Goals
- Keep Shadow Fang rule uncontested through intimidation and decisive violence
- Protect Kaelric, the manor, and the faction's local military posture
- Use Conclave-supplied technology to overwhelm under-equipped resistance

## Personality
Brutal, disciplined, and direct. Derrik does not waste time pretending cruelty is anything but useful.

## Secrets
- His mechanical arm is a Conclave gift and a mark of how deeply Stormhaven's criminal rule depends on outside tech
- He is more tactically aware than his reputation suggests and is not merely muscle

## Hooks
- A natural mid-to-late Stormhaven combatant for rebellion or manor-infiltration arcs
- Defeating him is a major symbolic blow against Shadow Fang rule
