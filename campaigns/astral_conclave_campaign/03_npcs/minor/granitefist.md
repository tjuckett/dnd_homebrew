# Granitefist

## Role
Warrior chieftain of [[Stonehearth]] and leader of its defenders.

## Goals
- Defend the village from outside threats
- Train elemental warriors to respond quickly to corruption, intrusion, or siege
- Maintain the physical strength and martial readiness of the community

## Personality
Imposing, direct, and steadfast. Granitefist respects strength, but values discipline and responsibility more than bravado.

## Secrets
He may distrust diplomacy with outsiders even when Eldara judges it necessary.

## Hooks
- Could test the players through trials of strength, endurance, or resolve
- A strong front-line ally if the village comes under attack
