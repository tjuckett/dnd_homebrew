# Ithilwen Moonshadow

## Role
Head priestess of the Temple of Eternal Winter in Wintervale.

## Traits
Wise, kind, steady, and spiritually respected.

## Functions
Blessings, rituals, winter rites, and counsel for travelers and townsfolk.

## Notes
Ithilwen helps keep [[Wintervale]] emotionally stable during long winters, failed harvests, and periods of renewed elemental fear. Her influence is rooted more in trust and endurance than in formal political authority.
