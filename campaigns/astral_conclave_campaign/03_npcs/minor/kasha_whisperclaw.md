# Kasha "Whisperclaw"

## Role
Escaped tabaxi test subject hiding inside the [[Null Bloom Facility]].

## Goals
- Keep surviving captives alive long enough for a real escape chance
- Sabotage Bloomwell's lower systems without drawing overwhelming retaliation
- Find or avenge the other tabaxi taken into Conclave custody

## Personality
Quiet, watchful, and deeply protective beneath layers of trauma. Kasha trusts action before words and measures every stranger by whether they endanger the vulnerable.

## Secrets
- She survived Conclave testing built around an echo-step collar and now knows maintenance routes the guards rarely monitor
- She has seen enough of the lower floors to understand that the extraction program is worse than most prisoners realize

## Hooks
- A stealth-focused ally who can guide the party through ducts, blind spots, and service corridors
- A witness whose testimony can tie Stormhaven disappearances directly to Bloomwell
