"If you are hearing this, then hopefully you are the heroes I've been waiting for. A strange old hermit told me to leave this message for you. I thought he was loony at first but he spoke of events that have yet to come to pass and knew things no one else could. So I'm leaving this in hopes he is correct. What else do I have to lose at this point"

"My name is Kira, I am a survivor from the age of technology."

"I'm not sure how much time I have, I have to go into hiding, so I'll get right to the point. Sealing the elemental planes at the end of the war had consequences we could not foresee. Our world is more dependent on the planes than we originally realized. Without the elements from the planes our world will slowly die as water dries up, fire stops burning, air stops moving. I tried to warn others but no one believes me. So I took it upon myself to break the planar seal and reopen the portals. I only hope the elementals won't start the war again."

"I designed a machine that can break the planar seal at the portal sites. This machine, once completed, can reopen the rifts between the material world and the Elemental Planes. But the energy required to achieve this is beyond what I ever imagined."

"My research led me down a dark path— Having no other sources of power I constructed collection stations across the Forbidden Realms, designed to siphon elemental energy from captured elementals. I had no other option. But it was not enough, each collection site couldn't capture enough energy. I do not think the elementals will ever forgive me, I regret my actions and am sorry for what I have done"

Kira’s voice falters slightly, and then she continues.

"I was about to give up hope when a group approached me in Wintervale, known as the Astral Conclave, they somehow knew who I was. They promised me the power I needed to finish my machine. They spoke of an astral comet that comes around during a celestial event, rich in energy, that could fuel the device. This is exactly what I needed. I designed a system to harness the comet and absorbs its energy into my machine. Too late did I realize their true intentions."

"Their goal is not to open the portals, but to wipe out the elementals instead. They somehow have plans for a machine that was designed during the war. This machine can wipe out an entire race at once across all planes of existence. Its intended use was to wipe out all elementals. But it was abandoned as it was considered genocide. I do not know how they found these plans, I thought them destroyed, but they plan to harness the comet’s power to use this machine to destroy all elementals everywhere, or worse a race they do not like here on the material plane. When I discovered their plans, I fled, cloaking this workshop and destroying all the lanterns that could lead back to it. I had no choice."

Kira’s voice grows more urgent.
"If you find this, know that time is running out. The Astral Conclave is preparing to capture the comet's power, and when it passes near the realm, they will enact their plan. You must stop them. If they succeed, not only will the elementals be destroyed, but the balance of the planes will collapse. The machine I have designed can save us, but it will need the comet's energy."

"In this workshop, you will find my journal—within its pages are all the details you need to harvest the energy of the passing comet. This comet holds enough power to fuel the machine I’ve designed, the Planar Rift Engine. If used properly, it will finally open the seals and allow the elemental energy to flow once again."

"But understand this: harvesting the comet’s energy is only the beginning. The machine must be physically taken to the elemental portal sites themselves, located on the continent across the seas. The journey will be perilous and the conclave will chase you, but it is the only way to break the seals at the rift points and open the gates to the Elemental Planes."

"I leave this task to you, because the hermit says you are the heroes of this age. Find the comet, stop the Conclave, and use the machine to reopen the rifts. The future of our world depends on it."
