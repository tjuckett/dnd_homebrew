To Whoever Finds This Note:



The Nexus is shut down, but it came at too great a cost. I managed to stop the catastrophic surge, but the damage... it’s beyond repair.

What we didn't anticipate—what no one could foresee—is how the elementals themselves were drawn to the Nexus. The Nexus was designed to channel and balance elemental energy, but something went wrong. When the corruption first started, the Nexus didn’t just malfunction—it began to pull elementals in, like moths to a flame. The closer they came, the more the Nexus fed on their energy, growing more powerful, more unstable.

The elementals were not attacking us out of malice. They were compelled—they had no choice. The energy of the Nexus corrupted them, twisted them into something violent, something mindless. They are not our enemies, but victims of the very device we built to control them.

Do not—under any circumstances—reactivate the Nexus. The energy will surge out of control, and it will once again draw elementals from every corner of this land. The pull will be irresistible. They will come, and they will be consumed by it, twisted into something worse than before. If the Nexus powers up again, the entire area will be overrun by elemental forces gone mad.

Leave this place in ruins, as it should be. Let the Nexus die in silence, and do not let the elementals suffer any further.

For the sake of all who remain, do not touch the Nexus again.



– High Engineer Lira Destra

May the elements find peace.
