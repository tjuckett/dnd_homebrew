Wondrous Item, rare (requires attunement by a druid)
A heavy, gnarled seed wrapped in living moss, pulsing with the rhythm of the land. It was once nurtured in the grove of a primordial archdruid.
Properties:
While attuned, you can cast Entangle at will without expending a spell slot.
Once per long rest, you may use an action to plant the seed in soil or wood. It erupts into a 10-foot-radius Heartroot Totem that:
Lasts for 1 minute or until destroyed (AC 15, HP 30).
Allies within the radius regain 1d4 hit points at the start of each of their turns.
The totem provides half cover and can absorb up to 30 points of damage dealt to allies (chosen by the totem’s creator).
If planted in corrupted or desecrated ground, the seed withers and cannot be used again until revitalized by a druidic ritual.
