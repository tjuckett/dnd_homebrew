The elemental orbs are created from condensing elementals down to their base energy and infusing that energy into an orb. This orb can enhance magic users with elemental magic or it can be incorporated into objects like weapons or armor to infuse them with elemental energy.

There are several quality levels of the orbs as the more energy is condensed the more powerful the orb is.

The orb can even be incorporated into automata for almost inexhaustible power supply.

These orbs were invented by Kira Emberweaver in an attempt to help augment the human armies against the machines during the Mecha war. Most were destroyed during the subsequent Elemental Invasion. It is unknown how many have survived and where they are.
