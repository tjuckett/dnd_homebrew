Magic Item: Eye of the Veil
Wondrous Item, very rare (requires attunement)
This refined platinum monocle bears a faint violet shimmer over its lens. Once worn by Lord Vancair Duskwright, it was a gift from his sovereign and enchanted through forbidden pre-war methods. Though subtle in design, it is anything but benign.Properties:
While attuned, the wearer gains:
Darkvision 60 ft. (or +30 ft. if already possessed)
Advantage on Insight and Perception checks involving sight
See Invisibility, once per short rest
Detect Thoughts, once per long rest (DC 15)
