Kira’s Journal: The Key to Unlocking the Planar Seals
1. Introduction: Kira’s Confession and Purpose
The first few pages of the journal serve as a personal confession, where Kira writes about her regrets for using elemental energy to fuel her work and her deep remorse for the suffering she caused. She explains that while her intentions were to free the elementals, her methods were flawed. She acknowledges that her only remaining hope is for someone else to complete her work, without causing further harm.

Kira admits to capturing and draining the life force of elementals to power her research.
She confesses that the energy from these collection stations is insufficient and unsustainable.
She writes that she has found a way to use a passing comet’s energy to complete her work and break the planar seals at the portal sites.
Kira emphasizes the urgency of the situation due to the Astral Conclave’s plans to use the comet’s energy for destruction rather than salvation.
2. Blueprints for the Planar Rift Engine
This section contains the intricate designs and schematics for the Planar Rift Engine, the machine Kira has designed to break the planar seals at specific elemental portal sites.

Elemental Stabilizers: The machine has four Elemental Stabilizers that align with the elemental planes—fire, water, earth, and air. Each stabilizer needs to be activated near its corresponding elemental portal for the machine to function properly.
Rift Core: The core of the engine is the Rift Core, a power module designed to channel immense amounts of energy. Kira initially intended to use elemental energy, but the comet’s astral energy will provide the necessary power.
Tuning Rods: These rods adjust the Planar Rift Engine’s frequencies to match the elemental seals. Kira writes that if the rods are not precisely tuned, the engine could fail or cause a dangerous backlash.
Transportation and Activation: The journal outlines the process of transporting the Planar Rift Engine to the elemental portals, where it must be assembled and activated to break the planar seals.
3. Notes on the Elemental Collection Stations
Kira includes an overview of the Elemental Collection Stations she built throughout the Forbidden Realms. These stations were designed to drain elemental energy and channel it into the Rift Core, but Kira acknowledges their limitations and the damage they cause to the elementals.

The collection stations are scattered across remote locations in the Forbidden Realms. They trap elementals and slowly siphon their life force.
The stations were originally intended to fuel the Planar Rift Engine, but the energy they collect is insufficient.
Kira includes maps of the station locations and instructions on how to disable them. She suggests that shutting down the stations might help restore balance to the elemental forces.
4. Comet Energy Harvesting
This section explains how Kira plans to harness the energy of a passing comet to power the Planar Rift Engine. The comet, rich with astral energy, provides the power required to activate the engine without harming the elementals.

Comet’s Energy: The comet contains immense astral energy that Kira theorizes can be harvested and stored in the Rift Core.
Energy Harvesting Process: The journal contains instructions for building a Comet Energy Collector to capture the comet’s energy as it passes.
Timing: The comet will soon pass near the realm, and the players must act quickly to harvest its energy.
5. The Astral Conclave’s Plan: Tractor Beams and the Astral Heart
In this section, Kira details her discovery of the Astral Conclave’s true intentions. They plan to use the comet’s energy for destruction, rather than freeing the elementals, by capturing it with tractor beams and storing it in a device called the Astral Heart.

Tractor Beams: The Conclave has developed tractor beams capable of pulling the comet toward their base. Kira includes notes on how these beams work and how they can be sabotaged.
Astral Heart: The Astral Heart is a massive device designed to absorb the comet’s energy and act as a miniature sun. The Conclave intends to use its power to eradicate the elementals.
Kira theorizes that the Astral Heart could be repurposed to power the Planar Rift Engine if the players can capture it before the Conclave.
6. The Elemental Portals and the Far Continent
The final section of the journal is dedicated to the elemental portals located on a distant continent. These portals are the key to breaking the planar seals and allowing the elementals to return home.

Portal Locations: The journal includes a map showing the locations of the elemental portals, each corresponding to a different elemental plane.
Activating the Planar Rift Engine: Once the Planar Rift Engine is powered, it must be transported to each portal site and activated to break the seals and reopen the elemental rifts.
Challenges at the Portal Sites: Kira warns that the portals are heavily guarded by elemental forces, and the players may face resistance from both elementals and remnants of the Conclave.
Personal Reflections and Final Thoughts
Throughout the journal, Kira’s personal reflections reveal her deep remorse and her desire to right the wrongs she has committed. She acknowledges that her methods have caused harm, but she believes that her research can still be used to save the elementals.

Her final entry reads:

"If anyone is reading this, know that I am sorry for what I’ve done. But I hope that you can finish what I could not. Free the elementals, and stop the Astral Conclave before they destroy everything."
