>> ENTRY 001: EMERGENCY EVACUATION ORDER



Author: Mayor Alaric Valens

Date: 208 Years Ago



MESSAGE:

--------------------------------------------------

The situation has become untenable. The city has come under 

attack from rogue elementals. These once-peaceful beings, 

now corrupted by some unknown force, have turned against us. 

They have breached the outer defenses and are wreaking havoc 

in the residential districts. The elementals are relentless, 

tearing through everything in their path—stone, metal, and 

even our citizens.



I am ordering the immediate evacuation of all districts. 

Citizens are to make their way to the eastern gate where 

transport is being arranged. Take only what you can carry. 

The Elemental Nexus is unstable, and we cannot guarantee how 

long the city will hold. May the elements guide us all in 

this dark hour.



--------------------------------------------------



>> ENTRY 002: NEXUS DAMAGE REPORT



Author: High Engineer Lira Destra

Date: 207 Years Ago



MESSAGE:

--------------------------------------------------

Mayor Valens, I regret to inform you that the situation is 

far worse than anticipated. During the elemental assault, 

several conduits connected to the Elemental Nexus were 

critically damaged. The Nexus is now operating at a 

dangerously unstable level. The energy output is erratic, 

and we are detecting significant fluctuations in the 

elemental balance. If these fluctuations continue, we risk 

a catastrophic meltdown that could obliterate the entire city.



I have dispatched teams to assess the damage, but I fear it 

may be beyond repair. We must initiate an emergency shutdown 

of the Nexus immediately to prevent further escalation. 

I will need your authorization to proceed with the shutdown 

sequence. Time is of the essence.



--------------------------------------------------



>> ENTRY 003: SHUTDOWN AUTHORIZATION



Author: Mayor Alaric Valens

Date: 207 Years Ago



MESSAGE:

--------------------------------------------------

High Engineer Destra, you have my full authorization to 

proceed with the emergency shutdown of the Elemental Nexus. 

The safety of our citizens must come first. I understand 

the risks involved in this process, but we have no other choice. 

We must prevent the Nexus from going critical at all costs.



I am coordinating with the evacuation teams to ensure that as 

many people as possible are clear of the city before the 

shutdown begins. I trust in your expertise to see this through. 

May the elements be with us.



--------------------------------------------------



>> ENTRY 004: FINAL ENTRY – NEXUS FAILURE IMMINENT



Author: High Engineer Lira Destra

Date: 207 Years Ago



MESSAGE:

--------------------------------------------------

Mayor Valens, I am afraid this will be my final report. 

The damage to the Nexus is too severe. The emergency 

shutdown sequence has failed—the Nexus is beyond our control. 

The elemental energy is surging uncontrollably, and we are 

witnessing rapid destabilization of the entire grid. 

The city is already beginning to tear itself apart. 



The rogue elementals continue their rampage, their actions 

seemingly in sync with the Nexus’s corruption. All remaining 

personnel are being ordered to evacuate immediately. 

I will stay behind to try and mitigate the damage, 

though I hold little hope.



I am truly sorry, Alaric. This city was a beacon of progress 

and harmony, and now it will be nothing but ruins. 

May the elements have mercy on us all.
