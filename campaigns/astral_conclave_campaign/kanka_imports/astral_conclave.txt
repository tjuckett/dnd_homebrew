Symbol: An intricately designed celestial clock with stars and planets in alignment.

Purpose: The Astral Conclave is an ancient and secretive organization of arcane scholars and magicians who have dedicated themselves to the study and manipulation of celestial forces. Originally formed to unlock the mysteries of the cosmos, the Conclave has evolved over the centuries, developing a hidden agenda that revolves around exploiting celestial phenomena for personal power and influence.

Goals:

The goals of the Astral Conclave are driven by their zealous pursuit of cosmic enlightenment and the advancement of their own power and influence. Here are their primary objectives:

Ascension to Celestial Heights: The ultimate goal of the Astral Conclave is to transcend the limitations of mortal existence and ascend to celestial heights, achieving a state of enlightenment and power beyond imagination. They believe that by unlocking the secrets of the cosmos and harnessing the energies of the stars, they can elevate themselves to a higher plane of existence.

Unraveling the Mysteries of the Cosmos: The Conclave seeks to unravel the mysteries of the cosmos, delving into arcane lore, planar exploration, and celestial magic to uncover hidden truths and ancient prophecies. They believe that by expanding their knowledge and understanding of the universe, they can unlock the secrets of existence itself.

Acquisition of Arcane Artifacts: The Conclave actively seeks out rare artifacts, ancient relics, and powerful magical objects that are believed to hold cosmic significance. These artifacts are coveted for their potential to enhance the Conclave's power and further their goals of cosmic enlightenment.

Establishing Dominion over Planes: With their growing mastery of planar magic and astral manipulation, the Conclave aims to establish dominion over distant planes of existence, bending them to their will and harnessing their energies for their own purposes. They view the planes as realms ripe for exploration and conquest, offering untold opportunities for cosmic advancement.

Expansion of Influence: The Conclave seeks to expand its influence and reach across the multiverse, recruiting followers, allies, and adherents to their cause. They use diplomacy, manipulation, and coercion to sway others to their side, seeking to build a vast network of supporters and agents who will further their agenda.

Eradication of Dissent: To maintain their dominance and suppress opposition, the Conclave ruthlessly eradicates dissent and quashes resistance from within and without. They view dissenters as heretics and traitors, willing to use any means necessary to silence opposition and maintain their hold on power.

In pursuit of these goals, the Astral Conclave stops at nothing, willing to commit acts of violence, manipulation, and betrayal to achieve their vision of cosmic enlightenment and transcendence.

Hiearchy:

High Archmagus: Seraphina Dawnweaver leads the Conclave with unwavering conviction, driven by her vision of transcending mortal limitations and ascending to celestial heights. While she espouses noble ideals of knowledge and enlightenment, her methods are often ruthless and unforgiving, prioritizing the advancement of the Conclave's goals above all else.

Grand Magisters: The Grand Magisters, appointed by the High Archmagus, are zealous enforcers of the Conclave's doctrine, willing to go to any lengths to further its agenda. They wield considerable influence within the organization, commanding loyalty and fear among their subordinates.

Starbound Zealots: Formerly known as Starbound Agents, these operatives are now fervent zealots who view the Conclave's mission as a divine mandate. They are willing to sacrifice everything in service to their cause, blindly following orders and committing acts of violence in the name of cosmic enlightenment.

Council of Ascendants: Renamed from the Council of Sages, this council comprises individuals who have achieved a higher state of enlightenment through their affiliation with the Conclave. They wield immense power and influence, guiding the organization's actions with an unshakable belief in the righteousness of their cause.

Guardians of the Celestial Order: The Guardians, once defenders of the Conclave's interests, have become enforcers of its will, ruthlessly quashing dissent and punishing those who oppose the organization's doctrine. They see themselves as arbiters of cosmic justice, willing to use force to maintain order in the pursuit of their goals.

Initiate Zealots: Initiates are indoctrinated from a young age into the Conclave's teachings, instilled with unwavering loyalty and devotion to its cause. They are trained to view outsiders with suspicion and to prioritize the Conclave's interests above all else, even at the cost of their own morality.

Through their zealous devotion to cosmic enlightenment, the members of the Astral Conclave have become morally ambiguous figures, driven by their fanaticism to achieve their goals by any means necessary. Their actions blur the line between right and wrong, leaving a trail of ethical dilemmas and collateral damage in their wake.

Astral Conclave’s Plot to Pull Down the Void Comet
Phase 1: Pulling Down the Void Comet
The Astral Conclave has discovered ancient plans for giant tractor beams capable of latching onto celestial bodies. By using Elemental Hearts as power sources, they intend to activate these tractor beams to pull the Void Comet from its natural trajectory, drawing it into proximity with the material plane.

Tractor Beams: These colossal structures are strategically positioned to channel massive amounts of energy from the Elemental Hearts, focusing their power on the comet. By adjusting the frequencies of the elemental energies, the Conclave plans to manipulate the comet’s movement, pulling it closer until it is within range of their final device.
Purpose: This step is necessary to harness the comet’s immense cosmic energy. Once the Void Comet is close enough, the Conclave can move to the next phase, which involves channeling this energy to activate their ultimate weapon.
Phase 2: Harnessing the Void Comet to Destroy All Elementals
At the heart of the Conclave's plan lies a devastating device initially conceptualized by the ancient artificer Kira. This machine, capable of channeling the power of the Void Comet, was initially intended as a last-resort weapon due to its capacity for complete annihilation.

The Device: Known as the Nullifier, this machine will utilize the Void Comet’s energy, which will be funneled through the Lantern and into the Elemental Hearts to focus its destructive power. Once activated, it can "void" entire categories of beings from existence.
Target: The Conclave's aim is to eliminate all elemental beings across all planes. They see the elementals as chaotic forces that disrupt their vision of order. By using the comet’s energy, the Conclave believes they can finally rid the material world of elemental influence.
The Risks: While they intend to focus the device’s power specifically on elemental beings, there is a dangerous possibility that it could overreach, destabilizing the material plane or even voiding all elemental connections across every plane. This could have catastrophic consequences for the balance of magic and natural forces in the universe.
Summary of Consequences and Stakes
If successful, the Astral Conclave will not only eradicate elemental beings from the material world but also risk throwing the entire cosmos into imbalance, potentially severing connections between planes and destabilizing the flow of elemental magic. The Void Comet’s energy is powerful and unpredictable, and harnessing it could lead to irreversible destruction.

The players must prevent the Conclave from drawing down the comet, stop the tractor beams, and dismantle or disable the Nullifier device before it can unleash the Void Comet’s power. With the fate of all elemental beings and possibly the stability of reality itself at stake, the players are the last hope to thwart the Astral Conclave's genocidal ambition.
